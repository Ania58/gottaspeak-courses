## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Past Simple vs Past Continuous (work context)**  
- I worked in a shop last summer.  
- I was serving customers when the phone rang.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I was work → ✅ I was working  
❌ He go to work yesterday → ✅ He went to work yesterday
