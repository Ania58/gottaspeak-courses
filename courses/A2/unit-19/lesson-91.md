## Grammar Input (5 min)
**Relative Clauses (people)**  
- A teacher is someone **who** helps students.  
- A doctor is a person **that** works in a hospital.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ He is a man which teaches → ✅ He is a man who teaches  

-------
