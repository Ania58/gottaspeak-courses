-------

## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Future Continuous**  
- At 10 a.m. tomorrow, I’ll be working.  
- This time next week, she’ll be travelling.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I will working → ✅ I will be working  
❌ Tomorrow I working → ✅ I will be working tomorrow
