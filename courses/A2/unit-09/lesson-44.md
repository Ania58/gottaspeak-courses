## Grammar Input (7 min)
**Reported Speech (statements)**  
- Direct: “I am tired.” → Reported: She said she was tired.  
- Direct: “I like pizza.” → Reported: He said he liked pizza.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ She said I am tired → ✅ She said she was tired
