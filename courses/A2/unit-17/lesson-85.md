## Idioms (3 min)
- home sweet home (happy to be at home)  
- feel at home (comfortable)  
- get on like a house on fire (very friendly)  
- bring the house down (make people laugh a lot)  
- hit home (be understood deeply)  
- safe as houses (very safe)
