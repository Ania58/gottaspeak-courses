## Grammar Input (5 min)
**Gerunds & Infinitives – review (technology focus)**  
- I like **using** my phone to study.  
- I want **to learn** coding.  
- I’m interested in **developing** apps.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I enjoy to use → ✅ I enjoy using  
❌ I want learning → ✅ I want to learn
