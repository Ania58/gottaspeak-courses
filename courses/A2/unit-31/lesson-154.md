## Idioms (7 min)
- **feel under the weather** – feel a bit ill  
- **in good shape** – physically healthy  
- **as fit as a fiddle** – very healthy  
- **take it easy** – relax and not work hard  
- **recharge your batteries** – rest to get energy again
