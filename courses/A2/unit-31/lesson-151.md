## Grammar Input (5 min)
**Review: Should / Shouldn’t / Ought to**  
- You should drink more water.  
- You shouldn’t eat too much sugar.  
- You ought to exercise regularly.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ You should to go → ✅ You should go  
❌ You ought eat → ✅ You ought to eat
