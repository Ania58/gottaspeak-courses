## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Could & Might**  
- She could play the piano when she was young. (ability in past)  
- It might rain tomorrow. (possibility)  

Common mistakes:  
❌ He coulds → ✅ He could  
❌ It can rain tomorrow → ✅ It might rain tomorrow  

-------
