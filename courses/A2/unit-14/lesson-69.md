Answer:

-------

## Idioms & Phrasal Verbs (7 min)
- shop around (compare prices)  
- pay off (finish paying)  
- cut back on (reduce spending)  
- splash out on (spend a lot at once)  
- run out of (use everything)  
- cost an arm and a leg (very expensive)
