## Warm-up (5 min)
Answer:
1. Are online shops as good as local shops?
2. Is shopping in your country as expensive as abroad?

-------

## Grammar Input (6 min)
**As…as / not as…as**  
- This shop is as cheap as that one.  
- My shoes are not as comfortable as yours.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ As more cheap as → ✅ As cheap as  

-------
