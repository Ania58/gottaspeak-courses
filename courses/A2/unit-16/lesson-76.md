## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Gerunds vs Infinitives**  
- like + -ing → I like swimming.  
- want + to + verb → I want to swim.  
- enjoy + -ing → I enjoy reading.  
- decide + to + verb → She decided to travel.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I enjoy to read → ✅ I enjoy reading  
❌ I want swimming → ✅ I want to swim
