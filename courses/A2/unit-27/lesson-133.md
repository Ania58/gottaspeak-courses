## Phrasal Verbs (7 min)
- **check out** (leave a hotel)  
- **run out of** (have no more of something)  
- **pick up** (collect someone or something)  
- **drop off** (take someone to a place and leave them)  
- **turn up** (arrive, often unexpectedly)  
- **sort out** (solve a problem)
