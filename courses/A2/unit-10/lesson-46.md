## Grammar Input (7 min)
**Passive Voice (Present & Past Simple)**  
- Active: People make smartphones in China.  
- Passive: Smartphones are made in China.  
- Active: They built this bridge in 2010.  
- Passive: This bridge was built in 2010.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ Is make → ✅ Is made  
❌ Was build → ✅ Was built
