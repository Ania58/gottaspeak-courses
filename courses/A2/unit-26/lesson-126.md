## Grammar Input (5 min)
**Gerunds & Infinitives – review**  
- I enjoy **spending** time outdoors.  
- She decided **to take** a break.  
- They hate **working** on weekends.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I enjoy to rest → ✅ I enjoy resting  
❌ She decided taking a break → ✅ She decided to take a break
