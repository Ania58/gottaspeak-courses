## Phrasal Verbs (7 min)
- burn out (get very tired from work)  
- chill out (relax)  
- take on (accept responsibility)  
- slow down (reduce speed, activity)  
- give up (stop doing something)  
- hang out (spend time with others)

## Skills – Reading (5 min)
**Text: How People Relax After Work**

Many people feel stressed after a long day at work. They take on too many tasks and forget to slow down. When this happens, they can burn out and feel tired all the time.

Some people relax by hanging out with friends, watching a film or going for a walk. Others chill out by listening to music or spending time alone. Taking short breaks during the day can also help.

Experts say it’s important not to give up healthy habits. Rest, free time and social contact help people keep their energy and feel better every day.
