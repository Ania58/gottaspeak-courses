-------

## Grammar Input (6 min)
**So / Such**  
- It was **so tasty**.  
- It was **such a good meal**.  

Rule:  
- so + adjective → so hot, so tasty  
- such + (a) + noun → such a surprise, such an idea  

Common mistakes:  
❌ such tasty → ✅ so tasty  
❌ so a big house → ✅ such a big house
