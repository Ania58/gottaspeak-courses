## Warm-up (5 min)
Answer:
1. What food is traditional in your culture?
2. What food rules do people follow in your family?

-------

## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Modals with Food Rules**  
- You must wash your hands before eating.  
- You should try this traditional dish.  
- You can’t eat pork here.  

-------

## Vocabulary (5 min)
Words: tradition, custom, festival, celebration, habit, taste, portion, meal.  

**Match (8)**  
A. a way of doing things that people follow for a long time → ______  
B. a small amount of food served for one person → ______  
C. the flavour of food or drink → ______  
D. a special event with music, food, or activities → ______  
E. something you do regularly, often without thinking → ______  
F. a special occasion when people enjoy something together → ______  
G. any food eaten at one time (breakfast, lunch, dinner) → ______  
H. old practice or belief from a group of people → ______
