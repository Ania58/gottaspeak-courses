## Idioms & Phrasal Verbs (7 min)
- a piece of cake (very easy)  
- spill the beans (tell a secret)  
- eat out (have a meal at a restaurant)  
- cut down on (reduce)  
- full of beans (energetic)  
- bring home the bacon (earn money)
