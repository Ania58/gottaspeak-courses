## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Too / Enough / So / Such**  
- It’s **too cold** to go out.  
- She isn’t **strong enough** to carry it.  
- It’s **so** beautiful!  
- It was **such** a nice day!  

Common mistakes:  
❌ Too much hot → ✅ Too hot  
❌ Enough big → ✅ Big enough  
❌ Such beautiful → ✅ Such a beautiful day  / so beautiful
