## Grammar Input (5 min)
**Comparatives + Intensifiers**  
- I’m **a bit** happier today.  
- She’s **much** more confident now.  
- This movie is **way** funnier than that one.  
- He’s **far** more patient than me.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ More easier → ✅ Easier  
❌ Very better → ✅ Much better
