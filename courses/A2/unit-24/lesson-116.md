## Grammar Input (5 min)
**Verbs of Feeling & Perception**  
- I feel tired.  
- You look happy today.  
- He seems worried.  
- She appears calm.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I feel myself tired → ✅ I feel tired  
❌ She looks like happy → ✅ She looks happy
