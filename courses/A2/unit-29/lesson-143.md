## Phrasal Verbs (7 min)
- **get along with** – have a good relationship  
- **look up to** – admire or respect  
- **fall out with** – argue and stop being friends  
- **grow up** – become an adult  
- **take care of** – help or protect  
- **run into** – meet by chance
