## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Past Simple vs Past Continuous**  
- I was walking in Paris when it started to rain.  
- We visited Spain last summer.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I was watch → ✅ I was watching  
❌ He go to Italy → ✅ He went to Italy
