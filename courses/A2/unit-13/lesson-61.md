## Grammar Input (7 min)
**Comparisons**  
- Trains are **cheaper than** planes.  
- Flying is **more comfortable than** driving.  
- This hotel is **as good as** that one.  
- The bus isn’t **as fast as** the train.  
- The more you travel, the more you learn.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ more cheap → ✅ cheaper  
❌ as tall than → ✅ as tall as
