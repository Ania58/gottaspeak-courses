## Grammar Input (6 min)
**Present Perfect with for/since**  
- I’ve lived here **for** 5 years.  
- She has worked there **since** 2018.  

Common mistakes:  
❌ I live here for 3 years → ✅ I have lived here for 3 years  
❌ I am here since 2010 → ✅ I have been here since 2010
