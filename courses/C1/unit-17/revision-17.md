# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- task aversion – dislike of starting or completing a particular task
- temporal discounting – tendency to prefer immediate rewards over future benefits
- emotional coping strategy – way of managing uncomfortable emotions
- self-efficacy – belief in your own ability to succeed
- self-fulfilling prophecy – situation where expectations influence the outcome
- sustained attention – ability to concentrate for a long period
- avoidance behaviour – actions taken to avoid uncomfortable situations
- immediate gratification – pleasure received instantly
- long-term benefit – positive result achieved in the future
- self-compassion – treating yourself with understanding instead of harsh criticism
