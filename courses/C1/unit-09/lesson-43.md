## 4. Grammar – Softening Criticism

Strong English does not always mean direct English.

Compare:

Blunt:
- He is selfish.

More tactful:
- He can be quite self-focused sometimes.

Blunt:
- She is difficult.

More nuanced:
- She can be a bit challenging to deal with in certain situations.

---

### Useful Language

- can be quite…  
- tends to…  
- may come across as…  
- sometimes struggles to…  
- isn't always…

## 7. Reading

Read the text.

People often focus on communication skills or shared interests when discussing relationships, yet emotional dynamics may matter just as much. Some individuals leave us feeling energised and understood, while others create tension or emotional fatigue.

Emotionally draining interactions do not always involve obvious conflict. Sometimes emotional exhaustion develops gradually through criticism, negativity or emotional imbalance.

Interestingly, difficult personalities are not necessarily intentionally harmful. Behaviour that feels demanding or frustrating may reflect insecurity, stress or poor communication habits.

Healthy relationships often depend not on perfect compatibility, but on mutual respect, emotional maturity and the ability to maintain boundaries.
