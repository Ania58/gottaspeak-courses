# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- take something with a pinch of salt – treat information with scepticism because it may not be completely true or reliable
- track record – history of someone's previous achievements or failures
- come to pass – happen or become reality (formal)
- dystopian – describing or believing in a future society characterised by suffering, injustice or oppression
- sequencing risk – danger that one change happens before another necessary change can occur
- workforce disruption – significant changes affecting employment and existing jobs
- get rid of – remove or eliminate something completely
- crop up – appear or happen unexpectedly
- labour transition – movement of workers from declining jobs into new professions
- technological uncertainty – unpredictability created by rapid technological development
