## 4. Grammar – Advanced Nominalisation (STRONG C1 EXTENSION)

At C1, we shift from verbs → complex noun structures:

Instead of:
→ Humans can adapt to difficult environments  

We say:
→ Humans demonstrate a remarkable ability to adapt to challenging environments  

---

### Key Transformations

- develop → the development of  
- adapt → the ability to adapt / adaptation  
- survive → survival / the capacity for survival  
- improve → improvement / enhancement  
- perform → performance

## 8. Reading

Read the text.

Humans are often perceived as physically weak when compared to other species. However, this perception overlooks a critical advantage: endurance. Rather than relying on short bursts of strength, the human body is designed to sustain effort over extended periods of time.

This ability is not accidental. It is the result of evolutionary adaptation, allowing humans to travel long distances, regulate body temperature efficiently, and conserve energy in ways that many animals cannot.

What is particularly striking is that this physical capability is closely linked to cognitive function. Movement, especially sustained movement such as walking, has been shown to enhance mental clarity and improve problem-solving.

In this sense, what appears to be a simple action may, in fact, represent one of the most sophisticated aspects of human design.
