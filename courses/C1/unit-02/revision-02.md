# Revision – Unit 2

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- understand and analyse a scientific explanation about human evolution  
- interpret complex cause-effect relationships  
- express ideas using advanced academic and natural language  
- connect physical movement with cognitive and social development  

---

## 1. Vocabulary Activation

2. Human bodies are __________ for movement rather than inactivity  
3. This ability gave humans a significant __________ over other species  
4. Movement can __________ cognitive performance  
5. Over time, small actions __________ and produce long-term results
