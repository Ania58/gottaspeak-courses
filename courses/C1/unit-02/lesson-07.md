## 4. Grammar – Advanced Comparison + Concession

We combine contrast and nuance:

- Although humans lack raw strength, they are far more capable of sustaining effort.  
- While animals may outperform humans in speed, humans excel in endurance.  
- Even though this seems like a disadvantage, it actually provides a long-term benefit.  
- Much as humans are physically limited, they compensate through efficiency.

## 8. Reading

Read the text.

At first glance, humans appear physically inferior to many animals. They lack speed, strength and natural weapons. However, this comparison overlooks a crucial advantage: endurance.

Humans are uniquely capable of sustaining moderate physical activity over long periods without overheating. This is largely due to efficient sweating and energy regulation, which allow the body to maintain performance even under stress.

While many animals rely on short bursts of power, humans rely on consistency. This ability to maintain effort, rather than maximise output, is what sets them apart.

In evolutionary terms, this difference proved decisive. The capacity to travel long distances without exhaustion enabled early humans to hunt, migrate and survive in changing environments.
