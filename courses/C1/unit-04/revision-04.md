# Revision – Unit 4

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- understand and analyse scientific explanations about the teenage brain  
- interpret cause-effect relationships in behaviour and development  
- express nuanced opinions using advanced structures  
- critically evaluate the impact of social media on mental health  

---

## 1. Vocabulary Activation

Complete naturally:

1. Teenagers are particularly __________ to external influence  
2. Social media can __________ reward systems in the brain  
3. This leads to increased __________ for stimulation  
4. Behaviour becomes __________ over time  
5. The brain may struggle with __________ and control
