## 4. Grammar – Causative Chains 

We describe multi-step processes:

- Social media provides unpredictable rewards, **which trigger dopamine release, which in turn reinforces repeated behaviour**.  
- The more frequently users receive feedback, **the more likely they are to return, further strengthening the habit loop**.

## 8. Reading

Read the text.

Digital platforms are designed to capture attention and maintain engagement. One of the key mechanisms behind this is the reward system, which responds to positive stimuli such as likes, comments and notifications.

What makes this system particularly powerful is its unpredictability. Rewards do not appear consistently, which encourages users to check repeatedly in anticipation of the next positive response.

Over time, this pattern creates a feedback loop. The brain begins to associate the platform with reward, reinforcing the behaviour and making it increasingly automatic.

As a result, what begins as casual use can evolve into dependency, especially in individuals whose regulatory systems are still developing.
