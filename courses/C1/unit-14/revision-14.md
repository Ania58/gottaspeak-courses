# Revision – Unit 14

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:

- discuss moral dilemmas and justice naturally
- evaluate revenge, loyalty and forgiveness
- distinguish between legality and morality
- express complex opinions about truth and honour

# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- loyalty – strong support and devotion
- honor – principles of dignity and respect
- revenge – harming someone in return for a wrong
- punishment – penalty for wrongdoing
- surrender – stop resisting and give oneself up
- revenge plot – secret plan to retaliate
- duty – moral or legal obligation
- deliberation – careful discussion before making a decision
- criminal – person who breaks the law
- legacy – how something is remembered
