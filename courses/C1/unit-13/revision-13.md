# Revision – Unit 13

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:

- discuss travelling and cultural experiences naturally
- talk about homesickness, belonging and lifestyle preferences
- analyse cultural differences and misunderstandings
- express opinions about what makes a place feel like home

# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- accommodation – place where someone stays
- scenic view – beautiful landscape or city view
- vibrant city – lively and energetic city
- multiculturalism – coexistence of different cultures
- skyline – outline of tall buildings in a city
- wind-chill factor – effect that makes the air feel colder
- tranquil – peaceful and calm
- unspoiled nature – natural environment unaffected by development
- jet lag – tiredness caused by travelling across time zones
- long-distance call – telephone call between distant places
