# Revision – Unit 6

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- analyse burnout and productivity culture critically  
- discuss meaningful work and impact using advanced evaluative language  
- reflect on ambition, achievement and emotional exhaustion  
- apply sophisticated structures naturally in discussion and writing
