## 4. Grammar – Rhetorical Qualification 

We soften and qualify arguments intelligently:

- To some extent, modern culture encourages people to define themselves through productivity  
- This is not to suggest that ambition is inherently harmful  
- It would be difficult to deny that constant optimisation creates pressure  
- While productivity itself is not the problem, the obsession surrounding it may be

## 8. Reading

Read the text.

Modern society increasingly encourages individuals to treat productivity as a measure of personal value. Achievement is often associated not only with success, but also with identity, discipline and self-worth.

As a result, many people experience guilt when resting or disengaging from work. Rather than viewing rest as necessary, they perceive it as unproductive or undeserved.

What makes this particularly difficult is the constant visibility of other people’s achievements. Social media reinforces comparison and creates the impression that everyone else is progressing faster, working harder or accomplishing more.

Over time, this pressure can become internalised. Productivity stops being a practical tool and becomes a psychological obligation.
