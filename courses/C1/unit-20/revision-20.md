# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- projection – psychological defence mechanism in which people attribute their own feelings or insecurities to others
- fundamental attribution error – tendency to explain other people's behaviour by their personality instead of external circumstances
- actor-observer bias – tendency to excuse our own behaviour because of circumstances while judging others by their character
- cognitive shortcut – simplified mental process used to make quick decisions
- unconscious conditioning – automatic influence of past experiences on present attitudes and behaviour
- defensive response – automatic emotional reaction to a perceived threat
- self-worth – sense of one's own value as a person
- social comparison – evaluating oneself by comparing with other people
- empathic perspective-taking – deliberate effort to understand another person's point of view
- reflective thinking – carefully examining one's own thoughts, assumptions and behaviour before reaching conclusions
