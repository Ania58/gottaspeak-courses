# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- expectations – beliefs about what people expect from someone
- isolation – feeling separated from others
- sense of belonging – feeling accepted and connected
- attention – interest shown by other people
- label – description that defines someone
- ordinary life – normal everyday life
- withdraw from something – gradually avoid people or situations
- fit in – feel accepted by others
- switch off – stop thinking or worrying
- ignorance is bliss – not knowing something can sometimes make life easier
