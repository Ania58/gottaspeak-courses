- Far from reducing loneliness, constant online engagement may intensify it

## 8. Reading

Read the text.

Modern technology has transformed communication, making interaction faster and more accessible than ever before. Yet despite this constant connectivity, loneliness has become increasingly common.

One explanation is that interaction and connection are not the same thing. People may exchange messages throughout the day while still lacking emotional closeness or genuine understanding.

What further complicates the issue is the performative nature of online communication. Individuals often present carefully constructed versions of themselves, making authentic vulnerability more difficult.

As a result, many relationships remain emotionally shallow. The appearance of connection exists, yet the deeper psychological need for belonging often remains unmet.
