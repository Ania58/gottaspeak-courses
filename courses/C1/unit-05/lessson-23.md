## 8. Reading

Read the text.

Parasocial relationships are emotional connections formed with public figures who are unaware of the individual’s existence. Although these relationships are one-sided, they can feel deeply personal and emotionally meaningful.

Social media has intensified this phenomenon by creating the impression of accessibility and intimacy. Influencers speak directly to audiences, share personal details and cultivate authenticity, making followers feel emotionally connected.

What is particularly complex is that these interactions can fulfil genuine emotional needs. For some individuals, online personalities provide comfort, familiarity and a sense of belonging.

At the same time, the emotional imbalance remains. While followers may feel deeply invested, the relationship itself is structured around distance, performance and consumption.
