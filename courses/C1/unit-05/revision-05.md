# Revision – Unit 5

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- analyse the psychological impact of online communication  
- discuss loneliness, belonging and online identity critically  
- apply advanced evaluative language naturally  
- reflect on emotional and social consequences of digital life  

---

## 1. Vocabulary Activation

2. Online communication can create emotional __________ rather than connection  
3. Social media often encourages carefully curated __________  
4. Constant comparison may reinforce emotional __________  
5. Many people struggle to remain fully __________ in real-life situations
