## 8. Reading

Read the text.

The human need for belonging is deeply rooted in psychology. Acceptance within a group has historically been linked to safety, survival and emotional stability.

As a result, social rejection can trigger powerful emotional responses. People who feel excluded often experience anxiety, insecurity and emotional withdrawal, even when the exclusion is subtle.

What makes modern social environments particularly challenging is the constant visibility of other people’s social lives. Online platforms intensify comparison and increase awareness of perceived exclusion.

In many cases, the fear of being rejected becomes so strong that individuals begin to suppress aspects of their identity in order to fit in.
