# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- attention crisis – widespread difficulty maintaining focus due to constant digital distractions
- compulsive checking – uncontrollable urge to repeatedly look at a device or application
- reward-seeking behaviour – actions motivated by the expectation of immediate pleasure
- emotional regulation – ability to recognise and manage emotional responses
- developmental milestone – important stage in psychological or physical development
- behavioural addiction – addiction involving repeated behaviours rather than substances
- cognitive vulnerability – increased susceptibility to negative effects on thinking and learning
- intermittent reinforcement – unpredictable rewards that strengthen repeated behaviour
- dependency cycle – repeating pattern in which reliance on something becomes stronger over time
- digital overstimulation – excessive exposure to digital information that overwhelms the brain
