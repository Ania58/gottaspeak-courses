# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- chinwag – a long and enjoyable conversation between friends
- enrich – improve something by adding value, knowledge or experience
- survival instinct – natural impulse to protect oneself from danger
- build bridges – improve relationships between people or groups
- tittle-tattle – unkind or trivial gossip about other people's lives
- talk over someone – interrupt or silence another speaker by speaking louder or continuously
- project (ideas) – express or communicate thoughts or opinions publicly
- listen to understand – listen with the goal of genuinely understanding another person's perspective
- reciprocal conversation – discussion in which both participants contribute and listen equally
- meaningful dialogue – conversation that leads to deeper understanding or stronger relationships
