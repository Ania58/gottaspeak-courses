## 8. Reading

Read the text.

Modern life exposes people to constant stimulation. Notifications, messages and endless streams of information compete for attention throughout the day.

Many individuals believe they are effective multitaskers. However, research suggests that switching repeatedly between tasks may reduce concentration and increase mental fatigue.

What makes distraction particularly challenging is that it often feels productive. People remain busy and mentally engaged while struggling to maintain deep focus on a single task.

Over time, constant interruption may contribute to fragmented attention, slower thinking and reduced cognitive recovery.
