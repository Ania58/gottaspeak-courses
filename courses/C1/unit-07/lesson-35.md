## 8. Reading

Read the text.

Artificial intelligence is transforming the way digital media is created and consumed. AI-generated images, voices and videos are becoming increasingly realistic, making it more difficult to distinguish authentic material from fabricated content.

One of the most concerning developments is deepfake technology, which allows realistic audiovisual manipulation to be produced quickly and cheaply. As a result, trust in digital evidence may gradually weaken.

What makes this issue particularly complex is that misinformation no longer depends solely on written claims. Visual and audio material, traditionally considered reliable forms of evidence, can now be artificially generated with remarkable accuracy.

Over time, this may contribute to a broader credibility crisis in which individuals become uncertain not only about specific information, but about the reliability of digital reality itself.
