# Revision – Unit 7

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- analyse misinformation and AI-generated media critically  
- discuss manipulation and persuasion using advanced evaluative language  
- express uncertainty and cautious interpretation naturally  
- reflect on trust, authenticity and digital reality
