# Revision – Unit 11

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- discuss decisions and their long-term consequences
- reflect on risk-taking and missed opportunities
- analyse regret and personal growth
- express complex opinions about life choices and change

---

# 1. Before Listening

Discuss.

1. Do people regret action or inaction more often?
2. Can one decision completely change a person's life?
3. Is it possible to have no regrets?
4. Why do people stay in situations they know are wrong?

---

# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- regret – sadness about a past decision or action
- hit rock bottom – reach the lowest point in life
- life-changing decision – a choice with major consequences
- perspective – a way of viewing a situation
- radical transformation – dramatic personal change
- habit patterns – repeated behaviours and ways of thinking
- flourish – grow, develop and succeed
- catalyst – something that causes change
- temporary situation – a situation that will not last forever
- fulfilment – deep satisfaction and meaning
