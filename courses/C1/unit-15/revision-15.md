# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- lean in to something – actively devote time and energy to something
- fare well – succeed or do well over time
- take a toll on someone – have a harmful effect on someone
- buffer someone from something – protect someone against something unpleasant
- bicker – argue repeatedly about unimportant things
- stale relationship – relationship that has become boring or routine
- hold a grudge – remain angry about something for a long time
- all walks of life – people from many different backgrounds
- dry up – disappear or stop completely
- smooth relationship – relationship without major conflicts
