# Lesson 71 – Growing Older: Blessing or Curse?

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- discuss ageing and life stages naturally
- express opinions about happiness and getting older
- compare advantages and disadvantages of different ages
- reflect on changing priorities and attitudes

---

# 1. Debate Starter

Read the statement.

## "People become happier as they get older."

---

## Initial Reaction

Choose your position.

- Strongly agree
- Mostly agree
- Unsure
- Mostly disagree
- Strongly disagree

Explain your answer.

# 3. Language From the Topic

- life stage – period in someone's life
- wisdom – knowledge gained through experience
- maturity – ability to behave sensibly and responsibly
- perspective – way of seeing life
- priorities – things considered most important
- insecurity – lack of confidence
- fulfilment – deep satisfaction and meaning
- ageing process – natural process of growing older
- self-acceptance – accepting yourself as you are

# 7. Reading

Read.

Many people fear getting older because they associate ageing with physical decline and lost opportunities.

However, studies suggest that happiness often increases with age. Older adults tend to worry less about social approval and focus more on meaningful relationships and experiences.

Ageing also brings greater emotional stability and self-awareness. Although growing older involves challenges, many people report feeling more content and less anxious than they did when they were younger.

Perhaps wisdom and happiness are not guaranteed by age, but experience can help people appreciate life differently.
