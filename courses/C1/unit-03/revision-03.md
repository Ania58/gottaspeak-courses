# Revision – Unit 3

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- understand and analyse a real-life AI voice scam  
- interpret intention, manipulation and emotional triggers  
- apply advanced language to discuss trust and deception  
- reflect critically on digital security and human behaviour  

---

## 1. Vocabulary Activation

Complete naturally:

1. The scammer tried to __________ trust by sounding familiar  
2. The situation created a strong sense of __________  
3. He didn’t question the story and was __________ by it  
4. The voice sounded completely __________  
5. The scam relied on emotional __________ rather than logic

## 11. Critical Thinking

Reflect:

1. Why do people trust voices so easily?  
2. Is emotional reaction stronger than logical thinking?  
3. Should people always verify identity, even in urgent situations?  
4. Will scams become more difficult to detect in the future?
