# 4. Language From the Listening

Work with these expressions.

- burnout – state of emotional and physical exhaustion caused by prolonged pressure  
- overload of work – excessive amount of work or responsibility  
- lose motivation – stop feeling driven or engaged  
- cynical – negative and distrustful attitude  
- happy-go-lucky – relaxed and naturally cheerful  
- hopeless – feeling that improvement is impossible  
- support system – family or people providing help  
- medication – medical treatment through drugs or medicine  
- meditate – practise calming and focusing the mind  
- distractions – things that interrupt attention or peace of mind
