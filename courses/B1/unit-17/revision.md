## Grammar Review ‚Äì Expressing Reasons and Contrast

We often explain **why something happens** or **why a decision is made**.

### Giving reasons
- **because** ‚Äì clear reason  
- **since** ‚Äì reason that is already known  

> I left early **because** I felt sick.  
> **Since** it was Sunday, the shop closed early.

### Showing contrast
- **although / even though** ‚Äì contrast, unexpected result  

> I worked from home, **although** I wasn‚Äôt in the office.  
> **Even though** he drives slowly, he wanted to go by car.
