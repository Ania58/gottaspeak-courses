## Language Focus ‚Äì Formal and Institutional Expressions

In formal English, speakers often:

- avoid personal language  
- use impersonal structures  
- choose careful, neutral wording  

### Common formal expressions

- in accordance with  
- be subject to  
- on behalf of  
- take into account  
- come into effect  
- with reference to  
- be entitled to  
- under the conditions of

## Reading ‚Äì Official Response to a Request

With reference to your recent enquiry, we would like to inform you that your request has been carefully reviewed by the relevant department.

Under the conditions of our current policy, access to the programme is granted only to applicants who meet the established requirements. These requirements are subject to regular updates and all decisions are made in accordance with internal procedures.

Please note that participation in the programme is subject to approval. Should your application be accepted, you will be informed of the next steps and the necessary documentation will be issued.

We appreciate your interest and thank you for your understanding. Should you require further clarification, please do not hesitate to contact the administration office.
