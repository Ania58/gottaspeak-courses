# 5. Reading – A Problem at Work

Yesterday morning, Daniel arrived at work early to prepare an important report. However, when he tried to open the document, the file was damaged.

At first he panicked, because the report was needed for a meeting that afternoon. Daniel asked his colleague for help, and together they tried to recover the file.

After checking several folders, they finally found a backup copy. Daniel quickly updated the missing information and finished the report just before the meeting.

The situation was stressful, but in the end everything worked out.
