## Idioms & Phrasal Verbs Focus: Hidden Meaning and Manipulation

### Idioms

- **read between the lines** – understand the real meaning, not just the words  
- **take something at face value** – accept something without questioning it  
- **twist the truth** – change facts to support a certain view  

### Phrasal Verbs

- **make up** (a story) – invent information  
- **leave out** – omit important details  
- **pick up** (information) – learn something informally  
- **look into** – investigate more deeply

## Reading: Comments, Context and Misunderstanding

> **Online comment thread**

**User A:**  
“This article clearly shows that the situation is getting worse. Anyone who disagrees is ignoring reality.”

**User B:**  
“You’re taking this completely at face value. The author left out key data.”

**User C:**  
“Exactly. If you read between the lines, you’ll see the article pushes a certain agenda.”

**User D:**  
“Or maybe you’re just twisting the truth to support your opinion.”
