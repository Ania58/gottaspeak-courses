# 4. Reading – A Memorable Journey

Read the text.

Last year, Julia decided to travel across northern Spain. At first she planned a simple trip with a few cities on her itinerary.

However, during the journey she discovered several small villages that were off the beaten track.

In one of them she came across a local festival with music and traditional food.

Julia ended up staying there for two extra days.

She says the experience was a hidden gem and one of the most memorable parts of her trip.
