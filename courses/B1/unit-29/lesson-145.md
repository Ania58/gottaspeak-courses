# Lesson 145 – Technology and the Future

## Warm-up – The Future

Discuss:

1. How will technology change our lives in the future?
2. What new technologies would you like to see?
3. Are you excited or worried about the future?

---

# Vocabulary – The Future of Technology

future  
innovation  
AI (artificial intelligence)  
automation  
robots  
digital world  
technology  

Example:

AI will change many industries in the future.

---

# Language Focus – Making Predictions

We use **will** to talk about future predictions.

Example:

Technology will make our lives easier.

Example:

People will use AI more in everyday life.

---

# Useful Expressions

I think… will…  
I believe… will…  
In the future…  
It’s possible that…  

Example:

I think robots will help people at work.

---

# Phrasal Verbs

**bring about**

cause something to happen

Example  
Technology will bring about many changes.

---

**come up with**

create or invent something

Example  
Scientists will come up with new solutions.

---

**take over**

replace or control something

Example  
Machines may take over some jobs.

---

**move forward**

progress or develop

Example  
Technology will move forward quickly.

---

# Idioms

**the future is now**

something that seemed far away is already happening

---

**a big step forward**

an important improvement

---

**change the way we live**

have a strong impact on daily life

---

**here to stay**

something permanent

---

**step into the future**

enter a new, modern stage

# Reading – The World of Tomorrow

Technology is developing faster than ever before.

In the future, many aspects of our lives will change.

Artificial intelligence will become more common, and robots may take over some jobs.

However, humans will still play an important role.

New inventions will bring about changes in education, healthcare, and communication.

Many experts believe that technology is here to stay and will continue to grow.

Although some people are worried about these changes, others see them as a big step forward.
