# Language Focus – Talking About Habits

We use the **present simple** to talk about daily habits.

Example:

I check my phone every morning.

Example:

She spends a lot of time online.

---

# Phrasal Verbs

**turn on / turn off**

start or stop a device

Example  
I turn on my laptop as soon as I wake up.

---

**log in / log out**

enter or leave an account

Example  
You need to log in to access the platform.

---

**scroll through**

move through content on a screen

Example  
I scroll through social media before going to sleep.

---

**check out**

look at something online

Example  
I checked out a new app yesterday.

---

# Idioms

**at your fingertips**

easily available

Example  
All the information is at your fingertips.

---

**a game changer**

something that completely changes a situation

Example  
Smartphones were a game changer for communication.

---

**can’t live without**

something very important in your life

Example  
I can’t live without my phone.

---

**part of everyday life**

something very common

Example  
Technology is part of everyday life.

---

**all the time**

very often

Example  
He is online all the time.

# Reading – Technology Everywhere

Today, technology is part of everyday life for most people.

We use our phones, computers, and other devices from morning until night.

Many people start their day by checking notifications and scrolling through social media.

During the day, we use digital tools for work, communication, and entertainment.

Information is always at our fingertips, which makes life easier.

However, some people feel they spend too much time online.

For them, technology is helpful, but it can also become a problem if they use it all the time.
