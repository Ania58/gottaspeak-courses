## Speaking Task – Solving a Difficult Situation

Choose **one situation from the listening** and explain:

- what went wrong
- what the person could do next
- what you would do in the same situation

Try to include:

- one expression of regret (*wish / if only*)  
- one solution using the **first conditional**  
- one phrasal verb from the unit

---

## Writing Task – Learning From Problems

Write **180–220 words**:

**“A difficult situation and how it could be solved.”**

Include:

- the problem
- possible regrets or mistakes
- a realistic solution

Use language from the unit:
- wish / if only
- first conditional
- at least **two idioms or phrasal verbs**
