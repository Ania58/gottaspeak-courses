# 4. Reading – Thinking About Your Future

Read the text.

Many people ask themselves an important question: *What kind of person do I want to be?*

Some people want to be helpful and kind. They try to support others and make people feel better.

Others want to be confident and brave. They like trying new things and taking risks.

Some people want to be creative. They enjoy developing ideas, creating art, or solving problems in new ways.

Life is not always easy. Sometimes plans fail and mistakes happen.

However, mistakes can help us learn and grow. They help us understand ourselves better and make better decisions in the future.

Small actions every day can slowly change who we become.
