## 6. Reading – Why Communication Matters

Read the text.

Communication plays a central role in all types of relationships, whether they are friendships, family connections, or romantic partnerships. People often assume that others understand what they think or feel, but this is not always true. Misunderstandings can easily happen when people do not express themselves clearly.

One common problem is avoiding difficult conversations. Many people prefer to ignore issues instead of talking about them. While this might seem easier in the short term, it can lead to bigger problems later. Small misunderstandings can grow into serious conflicts if they are not addressed early.

Another issue is not listening properly. Communication is not only about speaking, but also about understanding the other person. When people interrupt or focus only on their own opinions, it becomes difficult to build trust.

Healthy relationships require effort from both sides. This means being honest, listening actively, and trying to see things from the other person’s perspective. Even when people disagree, they can still respect each other and find a solution.

In the end, good communication helps people stay connected, solve problems more easily, and build stronger, more meaningful relationships.
