## 5. Reading – Problems in Relationships

Read the text.

Relationships can be challenging, especially when people have different expectations and opinions. Many problems appear when communication is not clear or when people are afraid to express their feelings honestly.

For young people, relationships with parents can be particularly difficult. Teenagers often want more independence, while parents worry about safety and responsibility. This can lead to disagreements about things like going out, meeting friends, or making personal decisions.

Another common issue is the fear of hurting someone’s feelings. People may avoid saying what they really think because they don’t want to cause conflict. However, this often makes the situation worse, as problems remain unsolved.

In friendships and romantic relationships, trust and respect are essential. Without them, even small misunderstandings can grow into bigger conflicts. On the other hand, when people communicate openly and try to understand each other, they are more likely to solve their problems successfully.

In the end, relationships require effort, patience, and good communication. Learning how to deal with problems in a calm and respectful way is an important skill for everyone.
