## 5. Reading – What Makes a Good Friend?

Read the text.

A good friend is someone who is honest, supportive, and easy to talk to.

It’s important to get along well and feel comfortable sharing your thoughts.

Some people are very outgoing, while others are shy, but personality differences don’t matter if there is trust.

Good friends listen to each other and try to understand how the other person feels.
