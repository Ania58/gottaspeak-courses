## 6. Reading – Who Inspires Us and Why?

Read the text.

People often look for inspiration in others. A role model can be someone famous, such as an athlete, entrepreneur, or artist, but it can also be a family member, teacher, or friend. What matters most is not fame, but the qualities a person represents.

Many people admire those who work hard to achieve their goals. Determination, discipline, and persistence are often seen as key traits of successful individuals. For example, someone who overcomes difficulties and continues to move forward can inspire others to do the same.

However, inspiration does not always come from big achievements. Sometimes it comes from everyday actions. A person who is kind, supportive, and reliable can have a strong influence on others. These qualities may seem simple, but they can make a real difference in people’s lives.

Another important factor is relatability. People often admire those they can identify with. If someone has had similar struggles or experiences, their success may feel more realistic and achievable.

In the end, role models help shape our values, decisions, and goals. They can motivate us to improve ourselves and become better people. The most important thing is not just to admire others, but to learn from them and apply those lessons in our own lives.
