## 6. Reading – Why Do Conflicts Happen?

Read the text.

Conflicts are a natural part of human relationships. No matter how close people are, disagreements will happen at some point. These conflicts can be caused by different opinions, misunderstandings, stress, or even small everyday situations.

One of the most common causes of conflict is poor communication. When people do not explain their thoughts clearly or fail to listen, problems can quickly grow. For example, a simple misunderstanding can turn into a serious argument if both sides react emotionally instead of calmly.

Another important factor is how people deal with their emotions. Some individuals tend to avoid conflict completely, while others may react too strongly. Both approaches can create problems. Avoiding issues can make them worse over time, while strong emotional reactions can damage relationships.

Learning how to manage conflict is an important skill. This includes staying calm, listening carefully, and trying to understand the other person’s point of view. It also means being honest but respectful when expressing your feelings.

In many cases, conflict can actually strengthen relationships. When people work through problems together, they can build trust and improve their communication. The key is not to avoid conflict, but to handle it in a healthy and constructive way.
