# 4. Reading – A Social Situation

Last Saturday, Julia attended a friend's birthday party. At first, she didn't know many people there, so she tried to break the ice by starting small talk.

She asked one person how they knew the host, and soon they were talking about travel and hobbies. A few minutes later, someone else joined the conversation.

At one point, the topic changed naturally when someone said, "Speaking of travel, has anyone been to Portugal?"

Later in the evening, Julia realized it was getting late. She smiled and said, "Anyway, I should get going. It was really nice talking to everyone."

The evening ended with several people promising to keep in touch.
