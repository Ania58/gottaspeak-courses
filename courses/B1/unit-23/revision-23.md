# Reading – Learning from Experience

Many people only understand the importance of discipline after making mistakes. Students sometimes believe that success will come easily, but reality often teaches a different lesson.

Some students struggle during their first year at university because they underestimate the workload. They may fall behind in assignments or fail an exam. However, these challenges often become valuable learning experiences.

After facing difficulties, students often become more organised and motivated. They learn how to manage their time, focus on their priorities and prepare for exams more effectively.

For many people, these experiences become turning points. What initially seemed like a failure later becomes a moment that helps them grow and succeed.
