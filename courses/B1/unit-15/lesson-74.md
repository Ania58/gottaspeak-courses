## Vocabulary Focus – Goals and Planning

### ➤ Key words

- goal – something you want to achieve  
- priority – the most important thing  
- deadline – final date  
- progress – movement forward  
- setback – a problem or delay  
- milestone – an important stage  
- outcome – result  
- task – a piece of work  

---

### ➤ Useful expressions

- set a goal  
- meet a deadline  
- make progress  
- focus on priorities  
- deal with setbacks  
- track progress  
- reach a target  

Examples:  
- We need to set clear goals.  
- She’s focusing on her priorities this week.  
- They made good progress last month.

## Grammar Practice – Choose the Best Option

1. He’s __________ a presentation tomorrow morning.  
   (giving / give)

2. They’re going to __________ their progress weekly.  
   (track / tracked)

3. If he studies regularly, he __________ improve faster.  
   (was / will)

4. The team __________ the deadline next Friday.  
   (is meeting / meets)

5. I think this plan __________ work well.  
   (am going / will)
