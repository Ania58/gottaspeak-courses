## Language Toolkit (Quick Review)

### Passive voice
- **Present simple:** am / is / are + past participle  
  *New measures are announced.*
- **Past simple:** was / were + past participle  
  *The decision was made yesterday.*

### Reporting vocabulary
- allegation, investigation, evidence, source, statement, responsibility

### Idioms & phrasal verbs
- read between the lines
- take something at face value
- point the finger at
- sweep something under the carpet
- carry out (an investigation)
- back up (a claim)

## Practice 2 – Complete the Report

Complete the text using the correct form.

An official statement __________ (release) earlier today.  
An investigation __________ (launch) after serious allegations __________ (make).  
No evidence __________ (confirm) at this stage, and the source __________ (protect).  
Authorities promised that responsibility __________ (take) once the facts __________ (verify).

## Reading: From Headline to Full Report

**Headline:**  
*Company hides important information*

**Report:**  
Following recent media attention, an internal investigation was launched. Certain details were left out in the initial communication, and responsibility was unclear. Officials stated that no attempt was made to cover up facts and that all claims would be backed up with evidence once the review is completed.
