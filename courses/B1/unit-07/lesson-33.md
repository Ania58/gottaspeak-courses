## Reading: Official Statement

> **Press Release**

An official statement was released earlier today following recent media reports. According to a reliable source, an internal investigation has been launched to examine the allegations. No confirmation has been provided at this stage.

Representatives stated that all available evidence will be reviewed carefully. The organisation accepted responsibility for informing the public and promised transparency throughout the process. Further details will be shared once the investigation is completed.
