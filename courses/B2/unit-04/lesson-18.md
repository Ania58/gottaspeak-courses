## 4. Grammar – Participle Clauses

We use participle clauses to make sentences shorter and more natural.

---

### 🔹 Basic idea

Instead of:
- Because I was tired, I went to bed early.  

👉 we say:
- Being tired, I went to bed early.  

---

### 🔹 Examples

- Feeling stressed, she took a break.  
- Having worked all day, he was exhausted.  
- Living in a big city, people often feel pressure.  

---

### 🔹 When do we use them?

👉 to show:
- cause  
- time  
- result

## 7. Reading – Managing Stress

Read the text.

Stress is a common part of modern life, but it can have serious effects if not managed properly. Many people experience stress due to work, personal responsibilities or lack of rest.

Working long hours, people often forget to take care of themselves. This can lead to burnout and health problems. Feeling overwhelmed, individuals may struggle to stay productive.

Having too many responsibilities, people may find it difficult to relax. As a result, they become less effective and more tired.

However, there are ways to manage stress. Taking regular breaks, exercising and maintaining a healthy routine can help improve well-being.

In the end, understanding stress is the first step to managing it effectively.
