## 3. Grammar Revision

### A. Verb Patterns

Complete the sentences.

1. I try __________ (improve) my sleep routine.  
2. She decided __________ (take) a break from work.  
3. He avoids __________ (check) his phone before bed.  
4. They managed __________ (reduce) stress levels.  
5. People tend __________ (ignore) early symptoms.  
6. I stopped __________ (drink) coffee in the evening.  

---

### B. Modals (Advice & Obligation)

Choose the correct option.

1. You __________ slow down if you want to avoid burnout.  
(a) had better (b) ought to  

2. You __________ ignore these warning signs.  
(a) should (b) had better not  

3. You __________ take breaks during the day.  
(a) need to (b) ought to  

4. You __________ push yourself too hard – it’s not healthy.  
(a) had better not (b) should  

5. You __________ improve your sleep routine.  
(a) ought to (b) had better  

6. You __________ take this seriously – it’s affecting your health.  
(a) should (b) need to  

---

### D. Collocations

Choose the correct option.

1. He needs to __________ a better routine.  
(a) do (b) build  

2. She wants to __________ real progress this year.  
(a) make (b) do  

3. You should __________ responsibility for your decisions.  
(a) do (b) take  

4. They are trying to __________ new habits.  
(a) form (b) make  

5. He managed to __________ his main goal.  
(a) achieve (b) make  

6. It’s important to __________ your skills over time.  
(a) develop (b) take  

---

### E. Cause & Effect

Choose the correct option.

1. Too much stress can __________ serious health problems.  
(a) lead (b) lead to  

2. Lack of sleep often __________ poor concentration.  
(a) causes (b) cause  

3. Bad habits can __________ long-term damage.  
(a) result (b) result in  

4. Regular exercise helps __________ illness.  
(a) prevent (b) preventing  

5. Poor diet __________ many health issues.  
(a) causes (b) results  

6. This situation may __________ negative consequences.  
(a) lead to (b) lead
