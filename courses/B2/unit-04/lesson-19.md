## 7. Reading – The Power of Small Habits

Read the text.

Many people believe that success comes from big changes, but in reality, small habits often have the greatest impact. Making progress does not require dramatic action. Instead, it involves consistent effort over time.

People who achieve their goals usually build strong routines. They develop habits that support their long-term success. For example, someone who exercises regularly may not see immediate results, but over time the benefits become clear.

Another important factor is patience. Progress is not always visible at the beginning. However, those who continue working towards their goals eventually see improvement.

It is also important to take responsibility for your actions. Without commitment, it is difficult to maintain positive habits.

In the end, success is not about doing everything perfectly. It is about making small improvements every day.
