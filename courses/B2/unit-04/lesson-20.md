## 4. Grammar – Cause and Result Language

---

### 🔹 Key structures

- lead to → Stress can lead to burnout.  
- result in → Poor diet results in health problems.  
- cause → Lack of sleep causes fatigue.  
- contribute to → Stress contributes to illness.  
- prevent → Exercise prevents health issues.  

---

### Practice

1. Lack of sleep __________ fatigue.

## 7. Reading – Lifestyle and Consequences

Read the text.

Lifestyle choices have a direct impact on health and well-being. Small daily habits can either improve or damage a person’s quality of life.

For example, poor diet and lack of exercise can lead to serious health problems over time. These habits often result in low energy levels and reduced concentration.

On the other hand, positive habits such as regular exercise and balanced nutrition contribute to better physical and mental health. They can also prevent long-term issues.

Stress is another important factor. High levels of stress can cause both physical and emotional problems. Managing stress effectively is essential for maintaining balance.

In the end, every choice has consequences. Understanding this can help people make better decisions and improve their overall lifestyle.
