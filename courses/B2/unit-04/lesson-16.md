## 4. Grammar – Verb Patterns (Extended)

Some verbs are followed by:
👉 gerund (-ing)  
👉 infinitive (to + verb)

---

### 🔹 Examples

- avoid **doing**  
- enjoy **doing**  
- decide **to do**  
- manage **to do**  
- tend **to do**  

---

### 🔹 Meaning differences

- stop doing vs stop to do  
- try doing vs try to do

## 7. Reading – Building Better Habits

Read the text.

Building healthy habits is not as simple as it seems. Many people start with strong motivation, but they struggle to stay consistent over time.

One common mistake is trying to change too much at once. People often decide to completely transform their lifestyle, which can be difficult to maintain. Instead, experts suggest focusing on small changes.

Another important factor is routine. Habits are easier to build when they are part of a daily structure. For example, exercising at the same time every day can make it easier to stay consistent.

It is also important to understand that failure is part of the process. People may miss a day or lose motivation, but this does not mean they should give up.

In the end, success comes from consistency, not perfection. Small actions repeated over time can lead to significant results.
