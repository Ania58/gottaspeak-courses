## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Emotions are rarely simple. People often experience multiple feelings at the same time, which can make them difficult to describe. Basic words are often not enough to capture emotional complexity.

Language allows us to express subtle differences. A person may feel slightly anxious, deeply frustrated or completely overwhelmed. These variations change how the situation is understood.

In many cases, emotions are not expressed directly. Instead, they are revealed through behaviour, tone or silence. Understanding these signals is an important part of communication.

The ability to describe emotions accurately makes language more powerful and more personal.
