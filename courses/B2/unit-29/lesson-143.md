## 3. Grammar – Modal Verbs for Interpretation

We use modals to show uncertainty:

- It might represent pain  
- It could symbolise identity  
- It may reflect personal experience  
- It can suggest emotional conflict  

---

Stronger:

- It must reflect something important  
- It seems to represent something deeper

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Interpreting meaning is not about finding one correct answer. Instead, it involves exploring possible explanations based on evidence and context.

Art often communicates indirectly. Symbols, colours and composition can suggest ideas without stating them clearly. This allows for multiple interpretations.

Viewers bring their own experiences and perspectives, which influence how they understand what they see. As a result, interpretation is always partly subjective.

However, this does not mean that any interpretation is valid. Strong interpretations are supported by details and logical connections.
