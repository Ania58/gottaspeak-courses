# Lesson 145 – Life and Reflection

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe a person’s life in a meaningful way  
- connect events with reflection and interpretation  
- use narrative and reflective language together

## 3. Grammar – Reflective Language

We combine narrative + interpretation:

- She went through a difficult period, which shaped her identity  
- He experienced loss, leading to a change in perspective  
- What makes her story powerful is how she transformed pain  
- Looking back, this moment seems crucial

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

A life story is more than a series of events. What makes it meaningful is how those events are connected and understood.

People often go through difficult experiences that shape who they become. These moments can act as turning points, influencing future decisions and actions.

Over time, individuals reflect on what they have experienced. This reflection allows them to find meaning in what has happened and to understand its impact.

In many cases, it is not the events themselves that matter most, but how they are interpreted and transformed.
