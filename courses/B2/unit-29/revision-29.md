# Revision – Unit 29

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- analyse how life experiences influence identity and art  
- describe emotional expression  
- interpret meaning in a balanced and precise way  

---

## 1. Vocabulary in Context

Complete naturally:

1. Her identity was shaped by her __________  
2. The experience had a strong __________ on her life  
3. Her paintings reflect deep __________  
4. The accident led to a major __________  
5. Her work expresses both pain and __________  

---

## 2. Structural Precision

Rewrite to sound more advanced:

1. She had a difficult life  
2. Her experiences changed her  
3. She expressed her emotions  
4. Her art shows her identity  
5. She became a strong person  

---

## 3. Emphasis

Rewrite using emphatic structures:

1. Her experiences shaped her identity  
2. Her pain influenced her art  
3. Her culture is important in her work  
4. Her emotions define her paintings  
5. Her life story is powerful  

---

4. Her work was shaped by __________

## 8. Listening – Life & Meaning

1. How did her early experiences shape her identity?  
2. Why did she turn to art?
