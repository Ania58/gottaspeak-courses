# Lesson 144 – Cause and Impact

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe cause and result in a natural way  
- explain how experiences influence outcomes  
- connect life events with personal development

## 3. Grammar – Cause & Result (Advanced)

Instead of simple:
❌ because / so  

Use:

✔ lead to  
✔ result in  
✔ give rise to  
✔ be shaped by  
✔ be influenced by  
✔ stem from  

---

### Examples:

- Her experience led to a change in her style  
- His work was shaped by personal struggles  
- This situation resulted in emotional tension  
- The idea stemmed from real events

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Life experiences often have a powerful influence on personal development. Events such as illness, loss or major change can shape how a person sees the world.

In many cases, difficult situations give rise to new forms of expression. Emotional pain may lead to creativity, while struggle can result in a deeper understanding of life.

These experiences are often reflected in a person’s work. Art, writing or other forms of expression can become a way of processing what has happened.

What matters is not only the experience itself, but how it is understood and transformed over time.
