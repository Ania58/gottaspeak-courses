# Revision – Unit 16 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- use key B2 grammar structures in context  
- describe language learning and bilingualism clearly  
- understand and analyse authentic listening  

---

## 1. Gerund vs Infinitive

Choose the correct form:

1. I didn’t mean (to say / saying) that.  
2. This will mean (to spend / spending) more time studying.  
3. I regret (to tell / telling) him the truth yesterday.  
4. She went on (to explain / explaining) the next point.  
5. The room needs (to clean / cleaning).
