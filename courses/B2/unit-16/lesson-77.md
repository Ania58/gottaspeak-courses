# Lesson 77 – Past Habits (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- distinguish between used to, would and past simple  
- describe past habits naturally  
- talk about past learning experiences  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. How did you learn languages in the past?
2. What habits did you have as a student?
3. Did your approach change over time?
4. What worked well and what didn’t?

---

## 2. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *When I first started learning English, I used to rely heavily on grammar exercises. I would spend hours filling in gaps and memorising rules, but I rarely spoke. At school, we used to have weekly tests, which made me focus more on accuracy than communication.*

*Later on, my approach changed. I started watching films and listening to podcasts. At first, I didn’t understand much, but I kept going. I would note down useful expressions and try to use them in conversations.*

*Looking back, I think I made faster progress when I stopped worrying about mistakes and started using the language more actively.*

---

## 3. Reading Comprehension

Answer:

1. What did the person do at the beginning?  
2. Why did they focus on grammar?  
3. What changed later?  
4. What helped them improve?  

---

## 4. Grammar – Key Differences

### used to
👉 past habits or states (no longer true)

- I used to study every day.  

---

### would
👉 repeated past actions (NOT states)

- I would practise every evening.  

---

### past simple
👉 finished action / general past

- I studied English at school.  

---

## 5. Choose the Correct Form

1. I (used to / would) be very shy when speaking.  
2. We (used to / would) have tests every week.  
3. I (used to / would) practise pronunciation every day.  
4. I (used to / would) enjoy learning vocabulary.  
5. I (used to / would) live in Spain for two years.  

---

## 6. Sentence Transformation

Rewrite:

1. I studied every evening.  
👉 I would __________  

2. I was shy when speaking.  
👉 I used to __________  

3. We had regular speaking classes.  
👉 We would __________  

4. I didn’t speak much before.  
👉 I didn’t use to __________

## 7. Error Correction

Find and correct:

1. I would be very shy as a child.  
2. I used to practise every evening and still do.  
3. I would live in France for two years.  
4. I didn’t would study much before.
