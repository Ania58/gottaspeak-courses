# Lesson 63 – Food Quality and Taste

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe food using a wide range of vocabulary  
- express opinions about food  
- talk about quality and freshness  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. What kind of food do you enjoy most?
2. What makes food taste good?
3. Do you prefer simple or complex dishes?
4. Have you ever eaten something disappointing?

---

2. bitter

5. spicy  
6. bland  

---

## 5. Language in Action

We describe food like this:

- It tastes amazing.  
- It looks delicious.  
- It feels too heavy.  
- It seems a bit overcooked.  

---

### Task

👉 Describe:

1. a meal you enjoyed  
2. a bad food experience  
3. your favourite dish
