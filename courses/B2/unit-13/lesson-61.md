# Lesson 61 – Food and Eating Habits

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe eating habits naturally  
- talk about food preferences  
- use common food-related expressions  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. What do you usually eat during the day?
2. Do you prefer home-cooked meals or eating out?
3. Do you have any unhealthy habits?
4. How often do you try new food?

---

## 2. Eating Habits in Practice

Read the situations:

1. Someone skips breakfast every day  
2. A person snacks constantly  
3. Someone cooks at home most of the time  
4. A person eats out several times a week  

---

### Task

👉 Describe each habit:

- healthy / unhealthy  
- regular / occasional  

👉 Then:
Which one is most common?

---

## 3. Useful Collocations

Match:

1. skip  
2. have  
3. eat  
4. follow  
5. cut down on  

---

a) a diet  
b) meals  
c) breakfast  
d) out  
e) sugar  

---

### Task

👉 Complete:

1. I try to __________ sugar during the week.  
2. I usually __________ breakfast.  
3. I don’t often __________ out.  
4. I try to __________ a healthy diet.  
5. I sometimes __________ snacks late at night.  

---

## 4. Language in Action

We talk about habits like this:

- I tend to eat quite late.  
- I usually skip breakfast.  
- I try to eat healthy during the week.  
- I avoid junk food most of the time.  

---

### Task

👉 Describe:

1. your daily eating habits  
2. something you try to improve  
3. something you do too often  

---

## 5. Phrasal Verbs – Food

Match:

1. eat out  
2. cut down on  
3. give up  
4. go off  

Meanings:

a) stop eating something  
b) eat at a restaurant  
c) reduce  
d) become bad (food)  

---

### Task

👉 Describe situations where:

- you eat in a restaurant  
- you reduce something unhealthy  
- food is no longer good  
- someone stops eating something  

---

## 6. Idioms – Food

Match:

1. have a sweet tooth  
2. be full of beans  
3. go bananas  
4. spill the beans  

Meanings:

a) become very excited or angry  
b) reveal a secret  
c) have a lot of energy  
d) love sweet food  

---

### Task

👉 Choose TWO idioms and create short examples  

---

## 7. Real-life Scenario

Situation:

👉 You want to improve your eating habits.

---

### Task

👉 What do you change?

- eat more regularly  
- cook more  
- avoid certain foods  

👉 Explain:
- why  
- what is difficult  

---

## 8. Speaking (1:1)

Discuss:

1. What are common unhealthy habits?
2. Is it difficult to eat healthy?
3. Do people care about what they eat?
4. What is more important: taste or health?

👉 Try to use:
- 2 phrasal verbs  
- 1 idiom  

---

## 9. Writing Task

Write **160–200 words**.

Topic:

**Your eating habits and how you would improve them**

Include:

- current habits  
- problems  
- changes  
- your opinion
