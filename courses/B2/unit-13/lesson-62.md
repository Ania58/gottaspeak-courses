# Lesson 62 – Cooking and Food Preparation

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe how food is prepared  
- use common cooking verbs  
- give clear instructions  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. Do you enjoy cooking?
2. How often do you cook at home?
3. What is the easiest dish to prepare?
4. What do you usually cook?

---

## 2. Cooking Actions

Match the verbs:

1. chop  
2. slice  
3. boil  
4. fry  
5. bake  
6. grill  

---

a) cook in hot oil  
b) cook in water  
c) cook in the oven  
d) cut into small pieces  
e) cook over direct heat  
f) cut into thin pieces  

---

### Task

👉 Complete the sentences:

1. First, __________ the vegetables into small pieces.  
2. Then __________ the water and add pasta.  
3. You can __________ the chicken in the oven.  
4. I usually __________ eggs in a pan.  
5. __________ the meat for a few minutes on each side.

## 4. Language in Action

We describe cooking like this:

- First, chop the vegetables.  
- Then add the ingredients.  
- After that, cook for 10 minutes.  
- Finally, serve the dish.  

---

### Task

👉 Describe how to prepare a simple dish  

(use: first, then, after that, finally)
