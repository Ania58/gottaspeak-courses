# Lesson 65 – Food Waste and Expiration

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- talk about food expiration and safety  
- describe food waste habits  
- make everyday decisions about food  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. Do you often throw food away?
2. Do you check expiration dates?
3. Have you ever eaten expired food?
4. Why do people waste food?

---

## 2. Expiration Basics

Match:

1. expired  
2. fresh  
3. spoiled  
4. safe to eat  
5. go off  

---

a) not good anymore  
b) recently made or bought  
c) still OK to eat  
d) past the date  
e) become bad  

---

### Task

👉 Complete:

1. This milk has __________, don’t drink it.  
2. The bread is still __________, you can eat it.  
3. This food has __________, it smells bad.  
4. The yoghurt is past the date but still __________.  
5. The meat has __________, we need to throw it away.

## 3. Everyday Decisions

---

- eat it

## 5. Language in Action

We talk about food safety like this:

- It’s still safe to eat.  
- You shouldn’t eat this.  
- It’s better to throw it away.  
- It doesn’t smell right.  

---

### Task

3. “This food looks fine.”
