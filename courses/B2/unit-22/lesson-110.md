# Lesson 110 – Ideal Living Conditions

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe ideal living conditions precisely  
- justify preferences clearly  
- evaluate accommodation options using structured language

## 3. Language Focus – Justification 

👉 instead of:
❌ I like it  

👉 use:

✔ What matters most is…  
✔ One of the key factors is…  
✔ The reason for this is…  
✔ This is important because…  
✔ What makes it suitable is…  

---

## 4. Precision Task

Choose the BEST option:

1. One of the key __________ in choosing accommodation is location.  
   (factors / elements / aspects)

2. What matters most is the level of __________.  
   (convenience / comfort / quality)

3. A good location can __________ daily life easier.  
   (make / allow / help)

4. Privacy is particularly __________ in shared accommodation.  
   (important / valuable / essential)

5. The overall living standard depends __________ several conditions.  
   (from / of / on)

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Choosing the right accommodation depends on a variety of personal factors. While some individuals prioritise location and accessibility, others focus on comfort and privacy.*

*One of the key aspects of a good living environment is balance. A place that is affordable but poorly located may not be practical in the long term. Similarly, a comfortable apartment that is too expensive may create financial stress.*

*Facilities and surroundings also play an important role. Access to transport, shops and quiet areas can significantly improve quality of life.*

*Ultimately, what makes a place ideal varies from person to person. The best option is one that aligns with individual needs, preferences and priorities.*
