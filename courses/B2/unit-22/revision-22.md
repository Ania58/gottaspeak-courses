# Revision – Unit 22

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- analyse real accommodation experiences  
- describe advantages and problems precisely  
- understand authentic student experiences  
- express evaluation and personal judgement  

---

## 1. Vocabulary Precision 

Choose the BEST option:

1. She was __________ about the type of accommodation she received.  
   (annoyed / disappointed / frustrated)

2. The location was __________ enough to make daily life easier.  
   (suitable / practical / convenient)

3. The environment was __________ due to constant noise.  
   (uncomfortable / stressful / unpleasant)

4. He __________ a large amount of debt during his studies.  
   (created / built up / developed)

5. The situation eventually became __________ to tolerate.  
   (impossible / unbearable / difficult)

---

5. move __________ different accommodation  

---

## 3. Precision in Context (Balanced Options)

Choose the BEST option:

1. She decided to __________ the situation rather than move out.  
   (accept / tolerate / put up with)

2. The accommodation was __________ but not ideal.  
   (acceptable / manageable / reasonable)

3. He didn’t __________ cooking and relied on ready meals.  
   (feel like / bother with / care about)

4. The housemates showed little __________ for others.  
   (awareness / consideration / respect)

5. The experience turned out to be more __________ than expected.  
   (challenging / demanding / difficult)

## 4. Sentence Transformation (Natural Language)

Rewrite:

1. The place was not nice and I didn’t like it.  
2. My flatmates were very loud and messy.  
3. I stayed because it was easier.  
4. I couldn’t study because of the noise.  
5. The situation made me want to leave.
