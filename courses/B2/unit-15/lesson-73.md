# Lesson 73 – Fame and Public Image

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe how people are perceived in public  
- talk about image vs reality  
- use natural B2 language to describe impressions  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. Why do public figures care about their image?
2. Do you think media shows people accurately?
3. Can someone control how others see them?
4. Do first impressions matter?

---

## 2. Describing Public Image

2. professional

5. authentic

## 3. Perception vs Reality

Look at the patterns:

- come across as…  
- be seen as…  
- appear to be…  
- seem to be…  

---

### Task

👉 Complete:

1. He comes across as __________, but in reality __________.  
2. She is seen as __________, although __________.  
3. He appears to be __________, but actually __________.
