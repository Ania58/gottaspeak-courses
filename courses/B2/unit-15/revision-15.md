# Revision – Unit 15 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe work in the film industry  
- talk about pressure, roles and teamwork  
- express opinions about working conditions  
- understand real spoken English in context  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. Do you think working in film is a dream job?
2. What challenges might people face in this industry?
3. Would you enjoy working on a film set?
4. What type of personality is needed for this job?

## 10. Listening Tasks

### Part 1 – General Understanding

1. Is working in the film industry described as easy or difficult?  
2. What is the speaker’s overall opinion?  

---

### Part 2 – Work Reality

1. Why is it difficult to find stable work?  
2. What happens between projects?  
3. What does the speaker say about job security?  

---

### Part 3 – On Set

1. What do junior workers often do?  
2. Why do they need to stay alert?  
3. What happens if they are not ready?  

---

### Part 4 – People

1. What are people in the industry like?  
2. Why can working with them be difficult?  
3. What attitude do you need to survive?  

---

### Part 5 – Community

1. What is positive about working on a film?  
2. How do people behave in a team?  
3. What does “warts and all” suggest?  

---

### Part 6 – Difficulties

1. Why is the beginning especially difficult?  
2. What problem does the speaker mention about language?  
3. What happens if you don’t understand instructions?
