# Lesson 74 – Work and Pressure in Show Business

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe pressure and demanding work conditions  
- explain challenges in high-pressure environments  
- talk about expectations and performance  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. Do you think working in show business is stressful?
2. What kind of pressure do actors face?
3. Is pressure always a bad thing?
4. Do people perform better under pressure?

---

## 2. Types of Pressure

Match:

1. time pressure  
2. public pressure  
3. financial pressure  
4. performance pressure  

---

a) expectations from audience/media  
b) limited time to complete something  
c) need to succeed or perform well  
d) money-related stress  

---

### Task

👉 Complete:

1. Actors often face __________ when working on major productions.

3. Deadlines create __________ during filming.  
4. Big budgets increase __________.

## 3. Describing Demanding Work

Look at the patterns:

- have to work long hours  
- be expected to perform well  
- struggle to keep up  
- manage to deliver results  

---

### Task

👉 Complete:

1. They have to __________ during filming.  
2. Actors are expected to __________.  
3. Many people struggle to __________.  
4. Despite the pressure, they manage to __________.
