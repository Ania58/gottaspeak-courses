# Lesson 72 – Behind the Scenes

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe what happens during production  
- talk about preparation and process  
- explain what working behind the scenes involves  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. What do you think happens before a film is released?
2. How much preparation do actors need?
3. Is filming more difficult than it looks?
4. What might go wrong during production?

---

## 4. Language in Action

We describe the process like this:

- They had to spend hours preparing.  
- The team went through several versions of the scene.  
- Everything had to be carefully planned.  
- Actors are expected to remember lines perfectly.  

---

### Task

👉 Describe:

1. how a scene is prepared  
2. what happens during filming  
3. what happens after filming
