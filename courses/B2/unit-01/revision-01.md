# Revision Lesson – Unit 1 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- revise key vocabulary and expressions from Unit 1  
- use mixed conditionals, verb patterns and habit structures accurately  
- understand spoken descriptions of personality  
- express opinions and reflect on behaviour and decisions  

---

## 1. Warm-up (Revision)

Answer the questions.

1. How has your personality changed over time?
2. What is a decision that influenced your life the most?
3. What habits would you like to change?
4. Do you find it easy to express your opinions clearly?

👉 Try to use:
- 1 idiom  
- 1 phrasal verb  

---

## 2. Vocabulary Revision – Unit 1

Complete the sentences using appropriate words.

1. He’s quite __________, so he doesn’t enjoy large social events.  
2. In __________, that decision was actually beneficial.  
3. I’m trying to __________ a bad habit.  
4. She managed to __________ her fears and speak in public.  
5. From my __________, this argument is not convincing.  

---

## 3. Grammar Revision

### A. Mixed Conditionals

Complete the sentences.

1. If I __________ (make) a different decision, I __________ (be) in a different situation now.  
2. If she __________ (be) more confident, she __________ (accept) the offer last year.  

---

### B. Used to / Would / Be used to / Get used to

Choose the correct option.

1. I __________ (used to / would) live abroad, but now I don’t.  
2. I’m still __________ (used to / getting used to) working under pressure.  
3. When I was younger, I __________ (would / used to) spend hours outside.  

---

### C. Verb Patterns (Gerunds vs Infinitives)

Complete the sentences.

1. I regret __________ (say) that.  
2. Try __________ (change) your routine.  
3. Remember __________ (send) the email.

## 5. Language in Use – Integrated Practice

Complete the sentences.

1. If I had taken that opportunity, I __________ now.  
2. I’m getting used to __________ in a fast-paced environment.  
3. He tends to __________ his emotions instead of expressing them.  
4. I understand your point. However, __________.  
5. Sometimes you need to __________ and try something new.

## 6. Speaking (1:1)

Discuss:

1. Which speaker from the listening are you most similar to? Why?
2. Do you believe personality can change over time?
3. How do your habits influence your decisions?
4. Is it better to take risks or play it safe?

👉 Try to use:
- 1 mixed conditional  
- 2 discourse markers  
- 2 phrasal verbs  
- 1 idiom

Write **200–250 words**.

**How personality and decisions shape our lives**

- how personality influences decisions
