## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Illegal wildlife trade is often discussed in terms of law enforcement, but the issue is far more complex. To put it simply, this is not just a legal problem — it is an economic and social one.

What this means is that focusing only on punishment will not solve the issue. Demand continues to drive the trade, and as long as buyers are willing to pay, suppliers will continue to operate.

That being said, enforcement still plays an important role. Without it, illegal networks would grow even faster. However, the point is that enforcement alone is not enough.

In other words, solving this problem requires a broader approach, including education, regulation and international cooperation.
