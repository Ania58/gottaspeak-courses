- describe treatment options

## 3. Language Focus – Recommendations (Real English)

👉 Instead of basic:
❌ You should rest  

👉 use:

✔ It’s recommended that patients rest regularly  
✔ Patients are advised to reduce activity  
✔ In some cases, treatment may involve medication  
✔ A common approach is to combine therapy with lifestyle changes

## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. Patients are __________ to monitor their symptoms carefully.  
   (recommended / advised / instructed)

2. Treatment may __________ a combination of medication and therapy.  
   (include / involve / consist of)

3. Recovery can be __________ and unpredictable.  
   (slow / gradual / delayed)

4. Some patients __________ to treatment more effectively than others.  
   (react / respond / adapt)

5. The condition may require long-term __________ rather than a cure.

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Treating chronic conditions is often more complex than treating short-term illnesses. In many cases, there is no single solution, and patients require a combination of approaches.*

*It is generally recommended that individuals follow a structured treatment plan, which may include medication, therapy and lifestyle adjustments. However, recovery is rarely immediate.*

*Some patients respond well to treatment, while others continue to experience symptoms despite medical support. This highlights the importance of identifying the underlying cause and adapting the approach accordingly.*

*Long-term management is often necessary, requiring patients to monitor their condition and make ongoing adjustments to their daily routine.*
