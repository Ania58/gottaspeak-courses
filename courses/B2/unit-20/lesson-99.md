# Lesson 99 – Living with a Condition

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe how conditions affect everyday life

## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. The condition can __________ daily functioning.  
   (affect / limit / restrict)

2. Patients often __________ a lack of understanding from others.  
   (experience / face / encounter)

3. Symptoms tend to __________ throughout the day.  
   (change / vary / shift)

4. The illness can __________ normal routines.  
   (interrupt / disturb / affect)

5. Many people struggle to __________ their responsibilities.  
   (maintain / continue / carry on)

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Living with a chronic condition can have a significant impact on daily life. Many patients report that their energy levels fluctuate, making it difficult to maintain a consistent routine.*

*One of the main challenges is unpredictability. Symptoms may vary in intensity, meaning that individuals cannot always plan their activities in advance.*

*The condition often interferes with work, social life and personal responsibilities. As a result, patients may feel isolated or misunderstood, especially when the illness is not visible.*

*To cope with these challenges, individuals develop strategies to manage their energy and adjust their expectations. In many cases, learning to recognise limits becomes essential for maintaining a reasonable quality of life.*
