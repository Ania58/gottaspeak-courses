## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Mental and physical health are closely connected, yet they are often treated differently. Physical conditions usually present visible symptoms, making diagnosis more straightforward.*

*In contrast, mental health issues are often less visible and can be more difficult to identify. This lack of visibility can lead to misunderstanding and, in some cases, social stigma.*

*Another key difference lies in how these conditions are experienced. While physical illnesses may limit movement or energy, mental conditions often affect emotional well-being and cognitive functioning.*

*Despite these differences, both types of health problems can have a serious impact on quality of life. Increasing awareness and improving access to treatment remain essential for both.*
