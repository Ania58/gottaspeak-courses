## 1. Vocabulary Precision 

Choose the BEST option:

1. The condition is often __________ because symptoms are not clearly visible.  
   (ignored / misunderstood / unnoticed)

2. Patients frequently __________ a lack of support from medical professionals.  
   (face / experience / encounter)

3. The illness can __________ daily functioning to a significant degree.  
   (affect / limit / interfere with)

4. Diagnosis may be __________ due to the complexity of symptoms.  
   (delayed / postponed / avoided)

5. The condition has a serious __________ on quality of life.  
   (impact / effect / influence)

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Chronic conditions can have a profound impact on an individual’s life, particularly when they are poorly understood. Patients often experience a wide range of symptoms, including extreme fatigue, cognitive difficulties and physical pain.*

*One of the major challenges is the lack of clear diagnosis. Because symptoms vary and are not always visible, patients may struggle to be taken seriously by medical professionals.*

*As a result, many individuals are left to manage their condition independently, developing their own coping strategies over time. This can lead to frustration, isolation and a reduced quality of life.*

*What makes these conditions especially difficult is their unpredictability. Patients may feel relatively stable one day and completely unable to function the next.*
