## 3. Language Focus – Cause & Effect (Natural Variation)

👉 Instead of repeating:
“X causes Y”

Use:
- X is often linked to Y  
- X can result in Y  
- X is one of the main reasons for Y  
- X tends to lead to Y

## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. Chronic stress is often __________ long-term health problems.  
   (connected with / responsible for / related to)

2. A sedentary lifestyle can __________ reduced energy levels.  
   (lead to / result in / cause)

3. Lack of sleep tends to __________ concentration and memory.  
   (affect / weaken / limit)

4. Poor eating habits are __________ weight gain and fatigue.  
   (associated with / linked to / tied to)

5. Irregular routines can __________ the body’s natural rhythm.  
   (disturb / break / interrupt)

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Modern lifestyles are often associated with a range of health problems. The combination of long working hours, lack of sleep and poor dietary habits can result in both physical and mental exhaustion.*

*Chronic stress is one of the main factors affecting overall well-being. It is often linked to sleep disturbances, reduced concentration and a weakened immune system.*

*At the same time, sedentary behaviour has become increasingly common. Many people spend long hours sitting, which can lead to reduced energy levels and long-term health risks.*

*What makes these issues particularly challenging is that they develop gradually. As a result, individuals may not recognise the impact of their habits until the symptoms become more serious.*
