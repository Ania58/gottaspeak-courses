   (develop / notice / experience)

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Chronic conditions are often difficult to diagnose because their symptoms can vary significantly between individuals. Patients may experience persistent fatigue, difficulty concentrating and muscle weakness, which can interfere with daily functioning.*

*In many cases, symptoms develop gradually, making them harder to recognise. Some people initially ignore the signs, assuming they are simply tired or stressed.*

*One of the biggest challenges is that these conditions are not always visible. As a result, patients often struggle to explain how they feel, especially when medical tests do not show clear results.*

*Living with a long-term condition requires ongoing management. Patients must learn to cope with symptoms, adjust their lifestyle and monitor their health carefully.*
