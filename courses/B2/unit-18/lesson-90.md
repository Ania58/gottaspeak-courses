## 3. Grammar – Future Forms (Meaning First)

### Will → prediction / opinion

- Jobs will change significantly.  

---

### Going to → planned / expected

- Companies are going to invest in AI.  

---

### Present continuous → arranged future

- I’m meeting my manager tomorrow.  

---

## 4. Choose the Best Option (Context-Based)

Choose the correct form. Both options may be possible — think about meaning.

1. I think remote work (will become / is becoming) more common in the future.  
2. Companies (will invest / are going to invest) in new technologies next year — it’s already planned.  
3. I (am starting / will start) a new role next Monday — everything is arranged.  
4. Jobs (will disappear / are disappearing) as automation increases right now.  
5. I believe people (will need / are going to need) new skills in the future.

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *The future of work is changing rapidly due to technological advancements and shifting expectations. Automation is already transforming industries, and many routine tasks are being replaced by digital systems.*

*At the same time, the demand for new skills is increasing. Employees are expected to adapt quickly and continuously develop their abilities. As a result, lifelong learning is becoming essential.*

*Remote work is also becoming more common, allowing companies to build global teams. However, this shift brings new challenges, such as maintaining communication and productivity.*

*Overall, the workplace is evolving into a more flexible and dynamic environment, where adaptability and innovation play a key role.*
