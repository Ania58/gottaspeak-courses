## 3. Grammar – Linking Words (B2)

### Contrast

- however  
- although  
- whereas  

---

### Addition

- moreover  
- in addition  
- not only…, but also…  

---

### Result

- therefore  
- as a result  
- this means that  

---

### Examples:

- He works long hours; however, he isn’t productive.  
- Although the workload is high, the team performs well.  
- She is efficient; therefore, she finishes tasks quickly.  

---

## 4. Choose the Correct Linker

1. He works a lot; (however / moreover) he is not efficient.  
2. (Although / Therefore) the task was difficult, she completed it.  
3. She is organised; (as a result / whereas) she meets deadlines.  
4. He prefers working alone, (therefore / whereas) she prefers teamwork.

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Productivity in the workplace depends on several key factors. Employees who are able to prioritise tasks and manage their workload effectively are more likely to meet deadlines and maintain efficiency. However, productivity is not simply about working longer hours.*

*Although some people believe that multitasking increases performance, research suggests that it often leads to mistakes and reduced focus. In contrast, individuals who concentrate on one task at a time tend to produce better results.*

*Moreover, the working environment plays a significant role. Open-plan offices can encourage collaboration; however, they may also increase distractions. As a result, companies are beginning to rethink how workspaces are designed.*

*Ultimately, productivity is influenced not only by individual habits, but also by organisational structure and expectations.*
