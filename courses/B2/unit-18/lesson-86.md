## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:

## 3. Grammar – Advanced Comparisons

### Basic comparative

- Modern offices are more flexible than traditional ones.  

---

### Intensifiers

- far more efficient  
- much more relaxed  
- slightly better  

---

### Negative comparison

- not nearly as structured as  
- not as formal as  

---

### Other structures

- one of the most innovative workplaces  
- by far the best option  
- increasingly common

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Modern workplaces are changing rapidly. Traditional offices, which were once highly structured and formal, are now being replaced by more flexible and collaborative environments. Many companies are introducing open-plan offices and offering flexible working hours.*

*These changes are often seen as more effective, as they encourage communication and creativity. Modern workplaces are also becoming less hierarchical, which allows employees to share ideas more freely.*

*However, not all changes are positive. Some people find open-plan offices distracting, and flexible schedules can make it harder to separate work from personal life. As a result, choosing the right working environment depends on individual preferences.*
