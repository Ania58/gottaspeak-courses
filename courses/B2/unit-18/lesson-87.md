## 7. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Company culture plays a crucial role in how employees perform and interact at work. A supportive environment, where communication is encouraged and employees feel valued, often leads to higher levels of job satisfaction and productivity.*

*In contrast, a high-pressure culture, which focuses heavily on results and competition, can create stress and reduce employee well-being. While some companies believe that this approach increases efficiency, it may also lead to burnout.*

*Modern organisations are increasingly focusing on professional development and flexible working conditions. Companies that invest in their employees tend to attract and retain talent more successfully.*

*Ultimately, company culture is shaped by leadership, expectations and the way decisions are made. A well-balanced workplace, which values both performance and well-being, is more likely to succeed in the long run.*
