## 7. Reading – Learning Through Experience

Read the text.

Travelling is often seen as a way to relax, but it can also be a powerful learning experience. Many people return from trips with new perspectives and a better understanding of the world.

Experiencing different cultures can challenge existing beliefs. People may realise that there is more than one way to live and think. This can lead to personal growth.

Sometimes, difficulties during travel can teach valuable lessons. Problems such as delays, misunderstandings or unexpected situations can help people become more flexible and independent.

Looking back on these experiences, many travellers say that the challenges were just as important as the positive moments.

In the end, travelling is not only about visiting new places, but also about learning and growing as a person.

---

### Reading Questions

1. Why is travelling a learning experience?
2. How can it change people’s perspective?
