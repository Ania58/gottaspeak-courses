## 4. Grammar – Future Forms in Context

We use different forms depending on intention and situation.

---

### 🔹 Present Continuous  
👉 fixed arrangements  

- I’m meeting my friend tomorrow.  

---

### 🔹 Going to  
👉 plans / intentions  

- I’m going to visit Italy next year.  

---

### 🔹 Will  
👉 decisions made now / predictions  

- I’ll book the tickets tonight.

## 7. Reading – Planning a Trip

Read the text.

Planning a trip can be both exciting and stressful. Some people prefer to organise everything in advance, while others enjoy being spontaneous.

Having a clear itinerary can make travelling easier. It helps avoid confusion and saves time. However, planning too much can also limit flexibility.

Many travellers prefer to leave some space for unexpected experiences. They may decide where to go or what to do at the last minute.

Technology has made planning much easier. People can book accommodation, check reviews and organise transport within minutes.

In the end, the best approach depends on personal preferences. Some people need structure, while others prefer freedom.
