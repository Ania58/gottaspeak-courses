## 4. Grammar – Narrative Tenses

We use different past tenses to tell a story clearly.

---

### 🔹 Past Simple  
👉 main events  

- We arrived late.  

---

### 🔹 Past Continuous  
👉 background / situation  

- People were waiting at the airport.  

---

### 🔹 Past Perfect  
👉 earlier past  

- We had missed the bus.  

---

### 🔹 Example

When we arrived at the station, the train had already left, and people were waiting for the next one.

---

### Practice

Choose the correct option.

1. When I arrived, they __________ dinner.

5. They __________ for hours before the bus arrived.

## 7. Reading – A Travel Story

Read the text.

Last summer, I went on a trip that I will never forget. I had planned everything carefully, but things didn’t go as expected.

When I arrived at the airport, I realised that I had forgotten my passport. I was standing in line when I suddenly noticed it was missing. I had left it at home.

I quickly called a friend, who brought it to me just in time. While I was waiting, I was feeling extremely stressed, thinking I would miss my flight.

Fortunately, I managed to check in at the last minute. The journey itself was smooth, but the beginning was unforgettable.

In the end, I learned that even well-planned trips can go wrong.

---

### Reading Questions

1. Why was the trip memorable?
2. What problem did the speaker have?
3. What happened at the airport?
4. How was the problem solved?
5. What did the speaker learn?
