# Revision – Unit 5 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- revise storytelling and narrative tenses  
- react naturally in conversation  
- compare cultures and express opinions  
- describe plans and reflect on experiences  

---

## 1. Warm-up (Revision)

Answer the questions.

1. What is the most unusual place you have visited?
2. Have you ever experienced culture shock?
3. Do you prefer planning trips or being spontaneous?
4. What is the most important lesson you’ve learned from travelling?

👉 Try to use:
- 1 contrast structure  
- 1 experience phrase

## 3. Grammar Revision

### A. Narrative Tenses

Complete the sentences using the correct form of the verb in brackets.

1. When we arrived, the tour __________ already __________ (start).  
2. I __________ (walk) through the market when I met a local guide.  
3. By the time we reached the station, the train __________ (leave).  
4. While we __________ (explore) the area, we found a hidden café.  
5. I __________ never __________ (see) anything like it before.  
6. They __________ (wait) for hours before the bus finally arrived.  

---

### B. Reacting Naturally

Complete the reactions.

1. “I lost all my luggage.”  
→ That must have been __________.  

2. “We found an amazing hidden place.”  
→ That sounds __________.  

3. “Our trip was cancelled at the last minute.”  
→ I can imagine how __________ that was.  

4. “We got stuck in a storm.”  
→ That must have been really __________.  

5. “Everything worked out in the end.”  
→ That’s great to __________.  

6. “We met some incredible people.”  
→ That must have been a __________ experience.
