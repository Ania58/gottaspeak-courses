## 4. Functional Language – Reacting

We use these expressions to react naturally.

---

### 🔹 Showing empathy

- That must have been frustrating.  
- I can imagine how stressful that was.  

---

### 🔹 Showing interest

- What happened next?  
- Really? What did you do?  

---

### 🔹 Reacting to stories

- It sounds like a difficult situation.  
- That must have been a great experience.  

---

### Practice

Choose the most natural reaction.

1. “I missed my flight and had to wait 10 hours.”  
(a) That must have been frustrating.  
(b) At least you had some time to relax.  

2. “We got lost in a foreign city.”  
(a) It sounds like a difficult situation.  
(b) That must have been quite an adventure.  

3. “I finally reached my destination after a long delay.”  
(a) That must have been exhausting.  
(b) At least you made it in the end.  

4. “We met amazing people during the trip.”  
(a) That must have been a great experience.  
(b) It sounds like the trip was really worth it.  

5. “I had to cancel my trip at the last minute.”  
(a) I can imagine how disappointing that was.  
(b) Maybe it was for the best.  

6. “We found a beautiful place nobody knew about.”  
(a) That sounds incredible.  
(b) You must have felt really lucky.

## 7. Reading – A Difficult Journey

Read the text.

Last year, I experienced one of the most stressful journeys of my life. Everything started to go wrong from the very beginning.

My flight was delayed for several hours, and when I finally arrived, my luggage was missing. I tried to stay calm, but it was difficult.

While I was waiting at the airport, I met another traveller who had the same problem. We decided to help each other and figure out what to do.

In the end, the airline found our luggage, and everything worked out. Although the experience was stressful, it also showed me how important it is to stay calm and flexible.

---

### Reading Questions

1. What went wrong at the beginning?
2. What problem did the speaker have?
3. Who did the speaker meet?
4. How was the situation resolved?
5. What did the speaker learn?
