# Lesson 119 – Risk and Uncertainty

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- express different levels of probability  
- evaluate risk in decision-making  
- describe uncertain situations precisely

## 3. Language Focus – Probability

👉 natural scale:

✔ It’s very likely that…  
✔ It’s quite likely that…

✔ It’s unlikely that…  
✔ It’s highly unlikely that…  

---

## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. It’s __________ that people will face financial difficulties.  
   (possible / probable / likely)

2. There’s a strong __________ that costs will rise.  
   (probability / chance / possibility)

3. It’s __________ that everything will go as planned.  
   (uncertain / unlikely / doubtful)

4. There’s a small __________ of success in this situation.  
   (likelihood / chance / probability)

5. The outcome is __________ at this stage.  
   (unpredictable / unclear / uncertain)
