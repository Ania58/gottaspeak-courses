# Lesson 116 – Planning for the Future

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- talk about the future with uncertainty  
- express probability and speculation  
- discuss long-term planning realistically

---

## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. It’s hard to __________ what the future will look like.  
   (guess / predict / expect)

2. There’s a strong __________ that costs will increase.  
   (probability / chance / possibility)

3. It’s __________ that people will work longer in the future.  
   (probable / likely / possible)

4. It’s __________ to say how things will change.  
   (unclear / impossible / difficult)

5. Things may __________ over time.  
   (develop / change / evolve)

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Planning for the future has become increasingly difficult due to economic uncertainty and rising living costs. Many people find it hard to predict what their financial situation will look like in the coming years.*

*While some individuals focus on saving money and building stability, others struggle to manage their current expenses. This creates a gap between those who can plan ahead and those who cannot.*

*In the long run, the ability to adapt may become more important than long-term planning itself.*

---

## 10. Reading Comprehension

Answer:

1. Why is planning difficult?  
2. What difference exists between people?  
3. What is becoming more important?
