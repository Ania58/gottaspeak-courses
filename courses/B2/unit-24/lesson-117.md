# Lesson 117 – Money and Saving

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe financial situations clearly  
- explain causes and consequences  
- talk about money in a realistic way

## 3. Language Focus – Cause & Consequence

✔ This leads to…  
✔ As a result…  
✔ One consequence is…  
✔ This means that…  
✔ Because of this…  

---

## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. High living costs can __________ financial stress.  
   (lead to / cause / create)

2. Many people struggle financially __________ rising expenses.  
   (as a result of / due to / because)

3. Without savings, people may __________ serious problems later.  
   (experience / face / deal)

4. This situation often __________ long-term instability.  
   (develops / results in / causes)

5. Poor financial decisions can __________ debt.  
   (result in / create / lead to)

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Many people today face increasing financial pressure due to rising living costs and unstable income. As a result, saving money has become more difficult.*

*Without proper planning, expenses can quickly exceed income. This often leads to debt and long-term financial problems.*

*At the same time, some individuals prioritise spending over saving, which can create further instability in the future.*

*Understanding the relationship between financial decisions and their consequences is essential for long-term stability.*

---
