## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. A growing number of people __________ financial difficulties later in life.  
   (deal with / face / experience)

2. One of the main __________ is the lack of savings.  
   (concerns / issues / problems)

3. It is becoming more __________ to retire comfortably.  
   (complicated / difficult / challenging)

4. Many people are not __________ for retirement.  
   (planned / prepared / ready)

5. In many cases, people __________ on external support.  
   (base / rely / depend)

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Retirement is becoming an increasingly complex issue in modern societies. Many people are not adequately prepared for life after work, mainly due to rising living costs and insufficient savings.*

*A growing number of individuals rely on pensions that may not be enough to maintain their standard of living. At the same time, longer life expectancy means that savings need to last for a longer period.*

*In many cases, people are forced to continue working beyond the traditional retirement age. This reflects a broader shift in how retirement is viewed in today’s world.*
