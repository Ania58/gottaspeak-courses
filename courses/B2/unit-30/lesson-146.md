## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- express ideas in a more personal and natural way

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Finding your voice is not about speaking perfectly, but about expressing yourself clearly and honestly. Many learners focus too much on avoiding mistakes, which can make their speech sound unnatural.

Confidence plays a key role in communication. When people feel comfortable, they are more likely to express their ideas fully and naturally.

What matters is not only what you say, but how you say it. Tone, structure and personal style all contribute to effective communication.

Over time, people develop their own way of speaking. This process requires practice, reflection and the willingness to take risks.
