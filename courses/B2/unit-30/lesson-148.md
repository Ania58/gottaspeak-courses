## 3. Grammar – Real-Life Conditionals

We use conditionals to talk about consequences:

- If people compare themselves too much, they lose confidence  
- If you focus only on success, you ignore progress  
- If expectations are too high, people feel pressure  

---

### Hypothetical reflection:

- If I didn’t compare myself to others, I would feel more confident  
- If people focused less on success, they might feel less stress

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Pressure and expectations are a normal part of modern life. Many people feel the need to succeed, often comparing themselves to others.

While some pressure can be motivating, too much of it can lead to stress and self-doubt. Social media has made this problem more visible, as people are constantly exposed to the success of others.

Expectations can come from many sources, including family, society and personal goals. When these expectations become unrealistic, they can negatively affect confidence and well-being.

What matters is finding a balance between ambition and self-acceptance.
