## 3. Grammar – Hypothetical Reflection

We reflect on the past:

- If I had made a different decision, things would have changed  
- If she hadn’t experienced that, she wouldn’t have grown

- If I were more confident, I would speak more  
- If people focused on learning, they would improve faster

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Personal growth often comes from experience. People learn by making decisions, facing challenges and reflecting on the results.

Mistakes play an important role in this process. They provide opportunities to understand what works and what does not. Without reflection, however, these lessons may be lost.

Looking back allows people to see patterns and recognise change. Over time, this leads to greater awareness and better decision-making.

Growth is not always immediate, but it becomes clear when we reflect on how far we have come.
