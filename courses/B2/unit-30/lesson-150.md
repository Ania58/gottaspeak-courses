## 3. Grammar – Reformulation

We clarify meaning:

✔ In other words…  
✔ What I mean is…  
✔ To put it differently…  
✔ What I’m trying to say is…  

---

### Example:

It’s difficult to explain.  
→ In other words, I don’t know how to describe it clearly.

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Clear communication is not about using complex language, but about expressing ideas in a way that others can easily understand. Many people make the mistake of trying to sound advanced, which can lead to confusion.

Precision means choosing the right words and structuring ideas logically. When a message is clear, the listener does not need to guess what is meant.

Reformulation is an important skill. It allows speakers to explain the same idea in different ways, which is especially useful when something is not understood immediately.

Effective communication depends on clarity, structure and the ability to adjust language to the situation.
