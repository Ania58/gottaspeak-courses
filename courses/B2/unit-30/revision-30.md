# Final Revision – B2 Course

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- express ideas clearly and confidently  
- analyse communication and personal development  
- use advanced B2 structures naturally  

---

## 1. Vocabulary in Context

Complete naturally:

1. She built her __________ through experience  
2. Social media can create __________  
3. Confidence develops over __________  
4. Her message is both personal and __________  
5. Success depends on both effort and __________  

---

## 2. Structural Precision

Rewrite to sound more natural and advanced:

1. She was shy but became confident  
2. She started making videos and became successful  
3. She had problems and then changed  
4. She speaks in a strong way  
5. Her story is inspiring  

---

## 3. Emphasis & Clarity

Rewrite using emphasis or reformulation:

1. Confidence is important  
2. Her message matters  
3. Communication is difficult  
4. People need support  
5. Expression is powerful
