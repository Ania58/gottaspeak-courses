## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. The best thing is to __________ calm and not react emotionally.  
   (remain / keep / stay)

2. One way to deal with it is to __________ the subject.  
   (switch / change / move)

3. There’s no point in __________ the situation worse.  
   (doing / making / turning)

4. It helps to __________ a way out in advance.  
   (prepare / think / plan)

5. What I usually do is __________ the conversation short.  
   (make / keep / cut)

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Social situations can sometimes be difficult to manage, especially when they become uncomfortable or awkward. In such cases, having simple strategies can make a big difference.*

*One common approach is to change the subject or keep the conversation light. This helps avoid tension and keeps the interaction moving.*

*Another strategy is to prepare an excuse in advance. This allows people to leave without creating conflict or drawing attention.*

*Ultimately, the best strategy depends on the situation. The key is to remain calm and adapt to what is happening.*
