---

- soften statements  
- sound natural

## 4. Precision Task 

Choose the BEST option

1. __________, I didn’t really enjoy the event.  
   (basically / honestly / actually)

2. I mean, it was fine, but __________ it was a bit awkward.  
   (seriously / in fact / to be honest)

3. __________, what do you do?  
   (well / so / anyway)

4. It was good, __________ nothing special.  
   (quite / just / really)

5. __________, I didn’t expect that at all.  
   (literally / honestly / basically)

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Small talk plays an important role in social interaction, even though it is often seen as superficial. Its main purpose is to create a comfortable atmosphere and avoid silence.*

*Successful small talk is not about saying something interesting, but about keeping the conversation flowing. People use fillers and simple questions to maintain interaction.*

*One of the biggest challenges is knowing how to continue when the conversation starts to fade. Changing the subject or reacting naturally can help keep things moving.*

*In many situations, small talk is less about content and more about social connection.*
