## 3. Grammar – Emphasis (Cleft Sentences)

👉 Instead of:

❌ Civilians suffer the most.  

👉 say:

✔ What matters most is the impact on civilians.  
✔ The people who suffer the most are civilians.  

---

### Structures:

- What matters most is…  
- The thing that concerns people is…  
- The main issue is…  
- What we often forget is…  

---

## 4. Choose the Best Option (Meaning-Based)

Choose the sentence that gives stronger emphasis:

1. (Civilians are affected / What matters most is the impact on civilians).  
2. (Infrastructure is damaged / The main issue is the damage to infrastructure).  
3. (People lack resources / What is most concerning is the lack of resources).  
4. (Aid is important / The key issue is the need for humanitarian aid).  
5. (Living conditions are bad / What people face is extremely poor living conditions).

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *The impact of conflict on civilians is often devastating. What matters most is not only the immediate danger, but also the long-term consequences for communities.*

*Many people are forced to leave their homes, resulting in large-scale displacement. The lack of access to basic resources, such as food and clean water, makes survival increasingly difficult.*

*The main issue is the breakdown of infrastructure, which affects healthcare, education and daily life. Without functioning systems, recovery becomes a slow and challenging process.*

*What is often overlooked is the psychological impact of conflict. Trauma and uncertainty can affect individuals for years, even after the conflict has ended.*
