## 3. Grammar – Advanced Linking (Argumentation)

### Contrast

- however  
- nevertheless  
- in contrast  
- whereas  

---

### Evaluation

- this suggests that…  
- this implies that…  
- it could be argued that…  

---

### Examples:

- The report seems objective; however, it may contain bias.  
- One source supports this view, whereas another challenges it.  
- This suggests that the situation is more complex.

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *Media coverage of conflict varies significantly depending on the source. While some outlets aim to present objective information, others may reflect a particular perspective or narrative.*

*In some cases, reports emphasise certain aspects of a situation while leaving others out. This can influence how audiences interpret events and form opinions.*

*It could be argued that the increasing speed of news production has affected the depth of analysis. As a result, complex issues are sometimes simplified or presented in a way that lacks context.*

*Nevertheless, access to multiple sources allows individuals to compare information and develop a more balanced understanding of global events.*
