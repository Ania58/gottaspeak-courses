## 5. Meaning-Based Task 

Choose the BEST option:

1. The __________ of tensions has raised concerns.  
   (increase / escalation / growth)

2. The __________ of resources has led to conflict.  
   (lack / shortage / absence)

3. The __________ of political instability is worrying.  
   (level / degree / amount)

4. The __________ of negotiations caused further problems.  
   (failure / collapse / ending)

5. The __________ of foreign intervention changed the situation.  
   (impact / effect / influence)

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *The causes of conflict are often complex and interconnected. The rapid escalation of political tension, combined with economic instability, can create a highly volatile situation.*

*In many cases, the competition for resources plays a key role. The growing demand for energy and raw materials has led to disputes between nations.*

*Another important factor is the failure of diplomatic negotiations. When communication breaks down, the risk of conflict increases significantly.*

*The involvement of external powers can also influence the outcome. While intervention may stabilise the situation, it can also lead to further escalation.*
