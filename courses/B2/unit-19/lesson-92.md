## 3. Grammar – Hedging (Core Skill)

👉 instead of:

❌ War will happen.  

👉 say:

✔ It seems that conflict is becoming more likely.  
✔ There is a risk that tensions could increase.  
✔ It is possible that the situation will worsen.  

---

### Useful Structures:

- it seems that…  
- it is likely / unlikely that…  
- there is a risk that…  
- it appears that…  
- it could be argued that…  

---

## 4. Choose the Best Option (Meaning-Based)

Choose the option that sounds more appropriate:

1. (War will happen / There is a risk that conflict could escalate) if tensions continue.  
2. (This will cause problems / It seems that this may lead to further instability).  
3. (The situation is dangerous / It appears that the situation is becoming increasingly unstable).  
4. (This is a serious issue / It could be argued that this is a serious issue).  
5. (This will definitely happen / It is likely that this will happen).

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *In many regions of the world, political tensions are increasing, although the exact outcome remains uncertain. It seems that several factors are contributing to this situation, including economic pressure and regional instability.*

*Experts suggest that there is a growing risk of escalation. However, it is difficult to predict how events will unfold. In some cases, diplomatic efforts appear to reduce tension, while in others they seem to have little effect.*

*It could be argued that the lack of clear communication between governments is making the situation worse. At the same time, there is still a possibility that negotiations will lead to a peaceful resolution.*

---

## 9. Reading Comprehension

Answer:
