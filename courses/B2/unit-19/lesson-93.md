## 3. Grammar – Formal Reporting

👉 Instead of:

❌ People think the situation is serious.  

👉 say:

✔ It is believed that the situation is serious.  

---

### Key structures:

- it is believed that…  
- it has been reported that…  
- it is widely expected that…  
- there is evidence to suggest that…  
- it is thought that…  

---

## 4. Choose the Best Option (Tone-Based)

Choose the more appropriate formal version:

1. (People think the situation is getting worse / It is believed that the situation is deteriorating).  
2. (They say the government made a mistake / It has been suggested that an error was made).  
3. (Experts think this will happen / It is widely expected that this will occur).  
4. (Some people say this is true / There is evidence to suggest that this is the case).  
5. (People think this is serious / It is thought that this is a serious issue).

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *In recent months, it has been reported that governments are taking a more active approach to international security. It is believed that new policies are being introduced in response to increasing global tensions.*

*There is evidence to suggest that economic factors are playing a significant role in decision-making processes. At the same time, it is widely expected that further measures will be implemented if the situation continues to develop.*

*It is thought that cooperation between countries remains a key factor in preventing escalation. However, it has also been suggested that differences in strategy may limit the effectiveness of these efforts.*
