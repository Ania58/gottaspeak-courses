## 3. Grammar – Present Perfect vs Past Simple

### Present Perfect
👉 past → result now

- English has changed a lot.  
- It has borrowed many words.  

---

### Past Simple
👉 finished time

- The Normans invaded England in 1066.  
- They introduced many French words.  

---

## 4. Choose the Correct Form

1. English (has changed / changed) a lot over time.  
2. The Romans (have introduced / introduced) Latin words centuries ago.  
3. People (have simplified / simplified) spelling in some cases.  
4. The language (has evolved / evolved) for hundreds of years.

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *English is often described as an irregular language, and there are good reasons for that. Over the centuries, it has evolved through contact with many different cultures and languages. For example, the Romans brought Latin, the Vikings added Norse influences, and the Normans introduced French vocabulary.*

*These historical events have affected the language we use today. English has borrowed thousands of words, which is why it contains such a wide variety of vocabulary. However, not all rules have changed in the same way. Some have remained consistent, while others have become simplified.*

*As a result, modern English is a mix of different systems. This is why spelling and pronunciation do not always match, and why learners often find the language confusing at first.*
