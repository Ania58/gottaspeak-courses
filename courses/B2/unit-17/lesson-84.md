## 3. Grammar – Present Continuous (Trends)

👉 used for ongoing changes

- English is changing rapidly.  
- New words are appearing every year.  
- People are using more informal language online.  

---

### Compare:

- English changes over time. (fact)  
- English is changing rapidly. (trend now)  

---

## 4. Choose the Correct Form

1. English (changes / is changing) quickly today.  
2. People (use / are using) more slang online.  
3. New expressions (appear / are appearing) every year.  
4. The language (becomes / is becoming) more informal.

## 8. Reading

Read the text:

👉 *English is changing faster today than ever before. One of the main reasons is the influence of technology and the internet. People are communicating more quickly and often prefer shorter, simpler forms of language.*

*As a result, abbreviations and informal expressions are becoming more common. Words like “LOL” or “DM” are now widely understood, even outside online contexts. New expressions are also constantly appearing and spreading across different platforms.*

*At the same time, some traditional forms of language are gradually disappearing. Certain formal structures are used less often, especially in everyday communication. This shows that English is continuously adapting to the needs of its users.*
