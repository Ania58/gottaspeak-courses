# Revision – Unit 17 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- explain historical language changes clearly  
- use key B2 grammar structures in context  
- understand and analyse authentic listening  

---

## 1. Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Choose the correct form:

1. English (has changed / changed) over many centuries.  
2. The Normans (have introduced / introduced) French words in 1066.  
3. Pronunciation (has evolved / evolved) over time.  
4. Printing (has helped / helped) spread English in the past.  

---

## 2. Passive Voice

Rewrite:

1. People introduced new spelling rules.  
👉 New spelling rules __________  

2. Scholars added extra letters.  
👉 Extra letters __________  

3. Different cultures influenced English.  
👉 English __________  

---

## 3. Reported Speech

Rewrite:

1. “Spelling doesn’t match pronunciation.”  
👉 Experts say that spelling __________  

2. “English has changed over time.”  
👉 They explain that English __________  

3. “Some letters were added later.”  
👉 He said that some letters __________  

---

## 4. Present Continuous (Trends)

Complete:

1. English is __________ (change) constantly.  
2. New words are __________ (appear) every year.  
3. People are __________ (use) more informal language.  

---

## 5. Cause & Effect

Complete:

1. English spelling is irregular because __________.  
2. Due to __________, pronunciation changed.  
3. This led to __________.  
4. As a result, __________.
