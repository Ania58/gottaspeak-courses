# Lesson 122 – Research and Evidence

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe research processes  
- understand how evidence is presented  
- use passive structures naturally

## 9. Reading

Read the text:

*In recent years, more attention has been given to how traditional medicine is studied and evaluated. In many cases, treatments that have been used for generations are now being tested using modern scientific methods.*

*In a typical study, a remedy is tested on a group of participants, and the results are compared with those of a control group. Data is collected over a period of time and analysed to determine whether the treatment has any real effect.*

*However, not all studies are equally reliable. Some are based on small samples, while others may not be properly controlled. As a result, conclusions can sometimes be misleading.*

*For this reason, it is important to examine how research is conducted before accepting its findings. Understanding the process behind the results can help people make more informed decisions.*
