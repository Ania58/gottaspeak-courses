# Revision – Unit 14 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe problems clearly and precisely  
- give natural, nuanced advice  
- explain issues and react appropriately  
- make decisions under pressure  

---

## 1. Warm-up (Discussion)

Discuss:

1. What kind of everyday problems happen most often?
2. Do people usually react calmly or emotionally?
3. What makes a problem difficult to explain?
4. Is it better to solve problems quickly or carefully?

---

## 2. Framing Problems

Complete the sentences:

1. I’ve been running into issues with __________ lately.  
2. Things haven’t been going as planned because __________.  
3. It seems to be getting worse whenever __________.  
4. I keep having problems with __________.  

---

## 3. Making Problems Clearer

Rewrite and expand:

1. My laptop doesn’t work.  
2. The service was bad.  
3. Something is wrong with my phone.  

👉 Add:
- when  
- how often  
- impact  

---

## 4. Giving Advice (B2)

Give advice for each situation:

1. Someone is always late  
2. A device isn’t working properly  
3. A plan keeps failing  

👉 Use:
- It might be worth…  
- You might want to consider…  
- One option would be…  

---

## 5. Explaining Problems

Complete:

1. I was expecting __________, but instead __________.  
2. What’s concerning is __________.  
3. The issue seems to be __________.  
4. This resulted in __________.  

---

## 6. Evaluating Options

Complete:

1. On the one hand __________, on the other __________.  
2. The main drawback is __________.  
3. This would mean __________.  
4. I’m leaning towards __________ because __________.
