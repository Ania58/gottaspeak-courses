## 4. Grammar – Future Forms (Advanced Use)

---

### 🔹 will (predictions / opinions)

- I think technology will improve our lives.  

---

### 🔹 going to (planned / expected)

- This trend is going to continue.  

---

### 🔹 Future Continuous

👉 actions in progress in the future

- People will be working remotely more often.  

---

### 🔹 Speculation

- It is likely that people will rely more on AI.  
- People might be using new technologies.

## 7. Reading – The Future of Work

Read the text.

The future of work is likely to change significantly in the coming years. Technology is expected to play a central role in shaping how people work and communicate.

Many experts believe that remote work will become more common. People will be working from different locations and using digital tools to stay connected. This may improve flexibility, but it could also create new challenges.

Automation is another key factor. Some jobs may disappear, while others will be created. Workers will need to adapt and develop new skills to remain competitive.

At the same time, companies will be focusing more on innovation and efficiency. This could lead to better working conditions, but also increased pressure.

In the long run, the future of work will depend on how society responds to these changes.
