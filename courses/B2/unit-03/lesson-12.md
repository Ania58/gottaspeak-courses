## 4. Grammar – Relative Clauses

---

### 🔹 Defining clauses (essential information)

- The app **that I use** is very helpful.  
- The person **who created it** is famous.  

---

### 🔹 Non-defining clauses (extra information)

- My phone, **which I bought last year**, still works well.  
- This app, **which is very popular**, is free.  

👉 we use commas 

---

### 🔹 Key differences

- Defining → necessary  
- Non-defining → extra

## 7. Reading – Technology in Daily Life

Read the text.

Technology plays a crucial role in everyday life. Devices that were once considered luxury items are now essential tools. Smartphones, which combine multiple functions, have changed the way people communicate and access information.

Many people rely on applications that help them organise their daily tasks. These tools, which are constantly being updated, make life more efficient. However, they can also create dependence.

Another issue is the speed of innovation. New technologies, which appear regularly, can make older devices obsolete very quickly. This leads to both excitement and frustration.

Despite these challenges, technology continues to improve quality of life. It offers opportunities that were not available before and helps people stay connected in ways that were once impossible.
