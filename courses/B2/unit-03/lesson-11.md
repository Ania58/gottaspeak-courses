## 4. Grammar – Quantifiers & Vague Language

We use these to avoid sounding too direct or absolute.

---

### 🔹 Quantifiers

- a number of people  
- a great deal of time  
- quite a few users  
- a large amount of content  

---

### 🔹 Vague Language

- People **tend to** share more online.  
- Social media **seems to** affect behaviour.  
- It **appears that** people trust influencers.  

---

## 7. Reading – The Impact of Social Media

Read the text.

Social media has become a central part of modern life. A large number of people use it daily to communicate, share ideas and stay informed. However, its influence on behaviour is not always positive.

Many users tend to compare themselves to others, which can lead to feelings of dissatisfaction. It appears that people often present an idealised version of their lives, which does not reflect reality.

At the same time, social media offers opportunities for connection and learning. Quite a few individuals use it to develop skills, build networks and express themselves creatively.

Privacy is another concern. A great deal of personal information is shared online, sometimes without people fully understanding the risks.

In the end, social media can be both beneficial and harmful. It depends on how people choose to use it.
