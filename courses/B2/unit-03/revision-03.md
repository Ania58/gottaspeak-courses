# Revision – Unit 3 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- revise key language related to society and technology  
- express opinions using hedging and cleft sentences  
- use relative clauses and future forms accurately  
- understand spoken content about technology and modern life

## 3. Grammar Revision

### A. Quantifiers & Vague Language

Complete the sentences.

1. A number of people __________ social media daily.  
2. There is a large amount of __________ online.  
3. People tend to __________ themselves to others.  
4. It seems that __________.  
5. Quite a few users __________.  
6. It appears that people __________.  

---

### B. Relative Clauses

Combine the sentences.

1. I use an app. It helps me organise my time.  
2. This is the website. I visit it every day.  
3. The company is successful. It created this software.  
4. I have a phone. I bought it last year.  
5. I met a person. He works in IT.  
6. This platform is popular. It has many users.  

---

### C. Cleft Sentences

Rewrite the sentences.

1. Technology changes our lives.  
2. People need better education.  
3. Social media affects behaviour.  
4. We need more balance in life.  
5. Society creates pressure.  
6. People care about money too much.  

---

### D. Hedging

Rewrite using hedging.

1. Technology is harmful.  
2. People believe everything online.  
3. This information is incorrect.  
4. Social media affects mental health.  
5. People trust influencers too much.  
6. Online content is always reliable.  

---

### E. Future Forms

Complete the sentences.

1. I think people __________ (use) more AI in the future.  
2. This trend __________ (continue).  
3. In 10 years, people __________ (work) remotely.  
4. It is likely that technology __________ (change) our lives further.  
5. People might be __________ (live) differently.  
6. Society __________ (become) more digital.
