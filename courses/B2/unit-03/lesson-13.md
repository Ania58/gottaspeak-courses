## 4. Grammar – Cleft Sentences

We use cleft sentences to emphasise information.

---

### 🔹 It is / was … that

👉 emphasises one element

- It is **money** that motivates people.  
- It was **technology** that changed everything.  

---

### 🔹 What … is / was

👉 emphasises the result or key idea

- What people need is **balance**.  
- What matters most is **time**.  

---

### 🔹 Why use them?

👉 to sound more natural and advanced  
👉 to structure arguments

## 7. Reading – What Really Matters

Read the text.

Modern society is often focused on success, money and achievement. However, it is not always clear whether these priorities lead to real happiness.

Many people believe that what matters most is financial stability. Others argue that it is relationships and personal fulfilment that truly define a meaningful life.

What is interesting is that people often realise this too late. They spend years chasing success, only to discover that they have neglected other important areas of their lives.

It is also important to consider the role of society. Expectations and pressure can influence how people define success and what they choose to prioritise.

In the end, what matters most may not be what society tells us, but what makes us feel fulfilled and balanced.
