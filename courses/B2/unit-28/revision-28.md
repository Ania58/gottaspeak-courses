# Revision – Unit 28

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- analyse a writer’s creative process  
- describe how stories are structured  
- evaluate writing techniques and decisions

## 8. Listening – Process Analysis

1. Why does he start with the ending?

## 9. Listening – Critical Thinking

Answer in full sentences:

1. Is this method effective for all writers?  
2. What are the advantages of knowing the ending first?  
3. What are the risks of this approach?  
4. How does time influence creative work?  
5. Would you use this method? Why / why not?
