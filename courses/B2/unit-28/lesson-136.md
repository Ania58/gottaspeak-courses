## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Writing is often seen as a creative process, but it also involves a series of decisions. Some writers prefer planning everything in advance, while others start writing without knowing where the story will go.

Many authors describe writing as rewriting. They begin with a rough draft and then spend a significant amount of time improving it. Small changes in structure or detail can completely transform a text.

Writers often develop habits that help them work more effectively. Some prefer writing at a specific time of day, while others avoid distractions by working in isolation.

What makes writing challenging is not just coming up with ideas, but deciding how to shape them. Choosing what to include and what to leave out is a key part of the process.
