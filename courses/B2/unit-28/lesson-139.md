## 3. Grammar – Natural Opinion Language

Instead of:
❌ I think that…

Use:

✔ This suggests that…  
✔ It seems that…  
✔ What stands out is…  
✔ One issue is that…  
✔ The problem is that…

## 9. Reading

Read the text.

Expressing opinions clearly is an essential part of communication. However, many learners rely on simple phrases that can make their speech sound repetitive or unnatural.

More advanced speakers vary how they introduce ideas. Instead of using the same structure, they highlight different aspects of their opinion, focusing on meaning, tone or effect.

At the same time, it is important to control how strong an opinion sounds. In some situations, direct language is appropriate, while in others, a more balanced or careful approach works better.

Effective communication depends not only on what is said, but how it is expressed.
