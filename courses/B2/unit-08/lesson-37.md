## 7. Reading – The Power of Persuasion

Read the text.

Persuasion plays a significant role in modern communication. From advertising to social media, messages are carefully designed to influence how people think and behave.

One common technique is emotional appeal. Messages often focus on feelings rather than facts, which can make them more effective.

Another strategy is social proof. People are more likely to believe something if they see that others believe it too.

At the same time, not all persuasion is negative. It can be used to encourage positive behaviour, such as healthy habits or environmental awareness.

However, it is important to recognise when persuasion becomes manipulation. Understanding these techniques allows people to make more informed decisions.
