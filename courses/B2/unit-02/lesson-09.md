## 4. Grammar – Modals of Speculation (Continuous)

---

### 🔹 Present Continuous Speculation

👉 guessing what is happening NOW

- He must be working late.  
- She might be preparing for the meeting.  
- They can’t be taking this seriously.  

---

### 🔹 Past Continuous Speculation

👉 guessing what was happening at a specific time

- He must have been working all night.  
- She might have been dealing with a problem.  
- They can’t have been paying attention.  

---

### 🔹 Key Difference

- He must have worked late. → finished action  
- He must have been working late. → ongoing process

## 7. Reading – What You Don’t See

Read the text.

People often judge success based on visible results. They see promotions, achievements and recognition, but they rarely see the effort behind them. What appears to be an overnight success is usually the result of years of hard work.

In many cases, individuals are working quietly behind the scenes. They might be studying, improving their skills or dealing with personal challenges. Others may assume that success comes easily, but this is rarely true.

It is also easy to misinterpret situations. For example, someone who seems unmotivated might actually be struggling with something difficult. Similarly, a person who appears confident might be dealing with uncertainty.

This is why it is important not to make quick judgments. People may be working harder than we realise, even if we cannot see it.

In the end, success is not always visible, and what people are going through is not always obvious.
