# Revision – Unit 2 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- revise key language related to work and career  
- use modal speculation (past & continuous)  
- form indirect questions naturally  
- discuss decisions and workplace situations fluently  

---

## 1. Warm-up (Revision)

Answer the questions.

1. What is the most important factor when choosing a job?
2. Have you ever made a mistake at work or school?
3. Do you prefer stability or flexibility in your career?
4. How do you usually solve problems?

👉 Try to use:
- 1 modal (must / might / should have)  
- 1 phrasal verb

## 3. Grammar Revision

### A. Present Perfect vs Continuous

Complete the sentences.

1. I __________ (work) here for five years.  
2. I __________ (work) on this project all day.  
3. She __________ (finish) the report already.  
4. We __________ (prepare) for this meeting since morning.  
5. They __________ (improve) their performance recently.  
6. He __________ (try) to solve this problem for hours.  

---

### B. Modals – Past & Continuous

Complete the sentences.

1. He must have __________ (forget) the meeting.  
2. She might have been __________ (work) late.  
3. They can’t have __________ (understand) the task.  
4. He must be __________ (work) right now.  
5. She might be __________ (prepare) for something important.  
6. They can’t be __________ (take) this seriously.  

---

### C. Indirect Questions

Rewrite the questions.

1. Where is the office?  
→ Could you tell me __________  

2. Is the job still available?  
→ I was wondering if __________  

3. What time does the meeting start?  
→ Do you know __________  

4. How long does the process take?  
→ Would you mind telling me __________  

5. Why did they cancel the meeting?  
→ Could you explain __________  

6. Who is responsible for this project?  
→ Do you know __________  

---

### D. Conditionals

Complete the sentences.

1. I won’t accept the job unless __________.  
2. We’ll succeed as long as __________.  
3. I’ll help you provided that __________.  
4. I’ll continue even if __________.  
5. We won’t finish on time unless __________.  
6. I’ll accept the offer as long as __________.

### Part 2 – Detailed Questions

1. Why is the speaker not willing to work for low pay?  
2. What does the speaker think about working long hours?  
3. Why is flexibility important?  
4. Why is the speaker frustrated about vacation time?  
5. How has working from home changed their life?  
6. What makes a job enjoyable according to the speaker?  
7. Why does the speaker feel insecure about their job?

1. The speaker thinks money is not important.
