## 4. Grammar – Present Perfect vs Present Perfect Continuous

---

### 🔹 Present Perfect  
👉 focus on **result**

- I have worked on many projects.  
- She has completed the report.  

---

### 🔹 Present Perfect Continuous  
👉 focus on **duration / activity**

- I have been working on this project for hours.  
- She has been preparing for the meeting all morning.  

---

### 🔹 Key Difference

- I have written 3 reports. → result  
- I have been writing reports all day. → process

## 7. Reading – Finding the Right Career

Read the text.

Choosing the right career is not always a straightforward process. Many people begin their professional lives without a clear idea of what they want to do. Over time, they gain experience, develop new skills, and gradually discover what suits them best.

In today’s fast-changing job market, flexibility is essential. People often change careers multiple times throughout their lives. This can be both exciting and challenging, as it requires constant learning and adaptation.

Another important factor is job satisfaction. While salary is important, many individuals prioritise meaningful work and a positive work environment. Feeling valued and motivated can have a significant impact on long-term happiness.

However, work can also become overwhelming. Long hours, high expectations and constant pressure may lead to burnout. This is why maintaining a balance between work and personal life is crucial.

Ultimately, a successful career is not only about achieving professional goals but also about personal growth and well-being.
