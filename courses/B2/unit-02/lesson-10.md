## 4. Grammar – Advanced Conditionals

---

### 🔹 unless (= if not)

- I won’t accept the job unless the salary improves.  

---

### 🔹 as long as / provided that (= condition)

- I’ll help you as long as you try.  
- We can proceed provided that everything is ready.  

---

### 🔹 even if (= contrast condition)

- I’ll continue even if it’s difficult.  

---

## 7. Reading – Making the Right Choice

Read the text.

Making decisions is a fundamental part of both professional and personal life. Some choices are simple, while others require careful consideration and analysis.

One common mistake is making decisions too quickly without evaluating all possible outcomes. On the other hand, overthinking can also be a problem, as it may lead to indecision.

Successful decision-making often involves balancing logic and intuition. People need to consider facts and risks, but also trust their instincts in certain situations.

Another important aspect is learning from past experiences. Mistakes can provide valuable insights and help improve future decisions.

Ultimately, there is no perfect decision. What matters most is the ability to adapt and respond effectively to the results.
