# Revision – Unit 27

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- analyse how translation affects meaning and style  
- evaluate language choices critically  
- describe linguistic differences precisely  

---

## 1. Vocabulary in Context

Complete naturally:

1. A translator must convey the __________ of the original text  
2. Some elements are lost due to linguistic __________  
3. Tone and __________ are often difficult to reproduce  
4. A translation may sound natural but differ in __________  
5. Cultural and linguistic __________ affect interpretation  

---

## 2. Structural Precision

Rewrite to sound more advanced:

1. Translation changes meaning.  
2. Languages are different.  
3. Some things are lost.  
4. The translator makes choices.  
5. The result depends on context.  

---

## 3. Emphasis

Rewrite using cleft sentences:

1. Meaning is the most important part.  
2. Tone changes the effect.  
3. Context creates difficulty.  
4. Language influences interpretation.  
5. Style makes the difference.  

---

## 4. Comparison & Evaluation

Expand each statement:

1. This version is better  
2. The translation is more natural  
3. The meaning is clearer  
4. The tone is different  
5. The sentence is effective  

Make each more precise.

## 7. Listening – Key Ideas

Complete:

1. Some elements are lost, especially __________  
2. The original language contributes to __________  
3. The translation may be beautiful in a __________ way  
4. One language is described as more __________  
5. Translation involves both loss and __________

## 8. Listening – Language Analysis

Explain:

1. Why is sound important in language?  
2. What is the difference between expressive and formal language?  
3. Why might a writer avoid using dialect directly?  
4. What role does culture play in language?  

---

## 9. Listening – Critical Thinking

Answer in full sentences:

1. Is it possible to fully preserve meaning in translation?  
2. What matters more: sound, meaning or style?  
3. Why might a translator change the original?  
4. What is gained by reading in your own language?  
5. Would you prefer original texts or translations?
