# Lesson 60 – Identity and Personal Growth

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- describe changes in identity over time  
- explain what influences personal growth  
- reflect on past experiences and development  

---

## 1. Warm-up

Discuss:

1. Do people change a lot over time?
2. What experiences shape a person the most?
3. Can people completely change who they are?
4. Is change always positive?

---

## 2. Change Over Time

Read the situations:

1. Someone becomes more confident after difficult experiences.  
2. A person changes their values over time.  
3. Someone stays the same despite new experiences.  
4. A person completely changes their lifestyle.  

---

### Task

👉 For each:

- what changed?  
- what caused the change?  

---

## 3. What Shapes Identity?

Look at the influences:

- relationships  
- environment  
- challenges  
- success and failure  
- personal decisions  

---

### Task

👉 Choose TWO that have the strongest impact  

Explain:
- why  
- how they affect people  

---

## 4. Language in Action

We describe growth like this:

- I became more confident over time.  
- I started to see things differently.  
- I learned to deal with difficult situations.  
- I realised what really matters.  
- I developed a different perspective.  

---

### Task

👉 Describe:

1. how you have changed over time  
2. what influenced that change  
3. what is different now  

---

## 5. Phrasal Verbs – Change

Match:

1. grow into  
2. move on  
3. go through  
4. build on  

Meanings:

a) experience something difficult  
b) develop gradually into something  
c) continue after something ends  
d) use something as a base to improve  

---

### Task

👉 Describe situations where people:

- change gradually  
- deal with difficult experiences  
- continue after change  
- improve based on past experience  

---

## 6. Idioms – Growth

Match:

1. change for the better  
2. learn the hard way  
3. grow as a person  
4. turn your life around  
5. see things in a new light  

Meanings:

a) improve significantly  
b) understand something differently  
c) improve emotionally  
d) learn through difficult experience  
e) change in a positive way  

---

### Task

👉 Choose TWO idioms and describe real-life situations  

---

## 7. Scenario – Personal Change

Situation:

👉 You realise you want to change an important part of your life.

---

### Task

👉 What do you focus on?

- small changes  
- big decisions  
- changing environment  
- changing mindset  

👉 Explain:
- why  
- risks  
- what works long-term  

---

## 8. Perspective Task

Read:

👉 “People don’t change unless they are forced to.”

---

### Task

1. Do you agree?  
2. Give an example  
3. What is the opposite view?  

---

## 9. Speaking (1:1)

Discuss:

1. What makes people change?
2. Can people fully change their personality?
3. Do difficult experiences always lead to growth?
4. What helps people improve?

👉 Try to use:
- 2 phrasal verbs  
- 1 idiom  

---

## 10. Writing Task

Write **180–220 words**.

Topic:

**How people change over time**

Include:

- examples  
- causes  
- challenges  
- your opinion
