## 4. Grammar – Conditionals in Real Use

---

### 🔹 Third Conditional  
👉 past → past result (imagined)

- If I had saved more, I wouldn’t have had financial problems.  

---

### 🔹 Mixed Conditional  
👉 past → present result

- If I had invested earlier, I would be richer now.  

---

### 🔹 Real-life use

👉 regret  
👉 analysis  
👉 reflection

## 7. Reading – Financial Mistakes

Read the text.

Many people make financial mistakes at some point in their lives. These mistakes often happen because of a lack of planning or knowledge.

For example, some individuals spend too much money without thinking about the future. If they had planned better, they might have avoided serious problems.

Others take risks without fully understanding the consequences. While some succeed, many experience losses.

However, financial mistakes can also be valuable learning experiences. People often become more careful and responsible after making poor decisions.

In the end, understanding past mistakes can help people make better choices in the future.

---

### Reading Questions

1. Why do people make financial mistakes?
2. What example is given?
3. What happens when people take risks?
4. What can mistakes teach us?
