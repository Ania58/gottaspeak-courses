## 7. Reading – Financial Responsibility

Read the text.

Financial decisions play an important role in everyday life. Many people face difficulties because they do not fully understand the consequences of their actions.

For example, spending too much money can lead to debt and stress. As a result, individuals may struggle to manage their finances.

On the other hand, careful planning can improve financial stability. People who think about their decisions are more likely to avoid problems.

However, not all decisions are easy. Unexpected situations can affect even well-planned budgets.

In the end, understanding the reasons behind decisions can help people make better choices and avoid negative outcomes.
