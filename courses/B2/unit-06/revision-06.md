# Revision – Unit 6 (B2)

## 🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, the student will be able to:
- talk about money and spending habits precisely  
- explain decisions and consequences  
- use passive voice and conditionals naturally  
- understand spoken content about alternative economic lifestyles
