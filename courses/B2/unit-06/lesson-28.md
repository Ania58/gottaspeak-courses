## 7. Reading – The Power of Advertising

Read the text.

Advertising plays a major role in modern society. Products are constantly promoted through different channels, including television, social media and online platforms.

Many advertisements are carefully designed to influence consumer behaviour. Bright colours, emotional messages and well-known personalities are often used to attract attention.

In recent years, digital advertising has become more personalised. Ads are often based on users’ online activity, which means people are shown products they are more likely to buy.

However, not all advertising is transparent. Some consumers are influenced without even realising it. This raises questions about ethics and responsibility.

In the end, advertising can be both helpful and misleading. Understanding how it works can help people make better decisions.
