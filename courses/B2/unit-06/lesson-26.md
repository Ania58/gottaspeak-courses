## 7. Reading – Spending Habits Today

Read the text.

Spending habits have changed significantly in recent years. While some people prefer to save money, others tend to spend more freely.

One major factor is lifestyle. People are often influenced by social media and advertising, which can encourage unnecessary spending. As a result, many individuals have fewer savings than in the past.

Another issue is impulse buying. Many people make purchases without thinking carefully, especially when shopping online. This can lead to financial problems over time.

However, there is also a growing awareness of financial responsibility. Some people are trying to manage their money more carefully by creating budgets and reducing expenses.

In the end, spending habits depend on personal choices and priorities.
